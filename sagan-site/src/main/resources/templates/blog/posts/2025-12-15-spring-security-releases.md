# Spring Security 2025-12 Releases

_Releases | Rob Winch |  December 15, 2025 | 0 Comments_

On behalf of the team and everyone who has contributed, I am pleased to announce the availability of Spring Security `7.0.1` and `7.0.2`. This release included back to back releases to fix a [bug](https://github.com/spring-projects/spring-security/issues/18315) in Spring Security 7.0.1.

The highlights of 7.0.1 include:

-   [Fixing MFA support in Authorization Server](https://github.com/spring-projects/spring-security/issues/18251)

For a complete list of changes, refer to the changelogs:

-   [`7.0.1`](https://github.com/spring-projects/spring-security/releases/tag/7.0.1)
-   [`7.0.2`](https://github.com/spring-projects/spring-security/releases/tag/7.0.2)
