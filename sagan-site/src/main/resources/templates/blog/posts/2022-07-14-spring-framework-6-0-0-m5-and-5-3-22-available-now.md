# Spring Framework 6.0.0-M5 and 5.3.22 available now

_Releases | Stéphane Nicoll |  July 14, 2022 | 0 Comments_

On behalf of the team and everyone who has contributed, I am pleased to announce that Spring Framework `6.0.0-M5` and `5.3.22` are available now.

Spring Framework `5.3.22` includes [45 fixes and improvements](https://github.com/spring-projects/spring-framework/releases/tag/v5.3.22) and is a recommended upgrade for all Spring production scenarios.

Spring Framework `6.0.0-M5` includes [85 additional fixes and improvements](https://github.com/spring-projects/spring-framework/releases/tag/v6.0.0-M5) specific to the 6.0 branch.
