# Spring Security 5.7.3 and 5.6.7 available now

_Releases | Josh Cummings |  August 15, 2022 | 0 Comments_

On behalf of the team and everyone who has contributed, I am pleased to announce that Spring Security 5.7.3 and 5.6.7 are available now. In both cases the releases are largely composed of dependency upgrades and minor fixes.

To learn more, please visit the [5.7.3](https://github.com/spring-projects/spring-security/releases/tag/5.7.3) and [5.6.7](https://github.com/spring-projects/spring-security/releases/tag/5.6.7) release summaries.
