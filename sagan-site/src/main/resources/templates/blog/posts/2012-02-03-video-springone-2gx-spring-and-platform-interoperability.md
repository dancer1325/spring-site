# Video: SpringOne 2GX - Spring and Platform Interoperability

_News | Adam Fitzgerald |  February 03, 2012 | 0 Comments_

[![SpringOne 2GX Video: Spring and Platform Interoperability](http://www.springsource.org/files/SpringOne2GX2012SpringAndPlatformInteroperability.png)](http://www.infoq.com/presentations/Spring-and-Platform-Interoperability)

In another session from SpringOne 2GX 2011 SpringSource engineer Stephen Bohlen examines [Spring and Platform Interoperability](http://www.infoq.com/presentations/Spring-and-Platform-Interoperability). Stephen analyzes different approaches to Java and .NET interoperability, discusses how Spring can help and then provides a detailed sample application that expresses some of the best practices. Stephen's work on the [Spring.NET project](http://springframework.net/) gives him the perfect perspective on how to address these sorts of problems.

Many thanks to [InfoQ](http://www.infoq.com) for coming to Chicago to record so many of the fantastic [SpringOne 2GX presentations](http://www.infoq.com/springone_2gx_2011/).
