# Spring Batch 4.3.4 and 4.2.8 available now!

_Releases | Mahmoud Ben Hassine |  November 17, 2021 | 0 Comments_

I am pleased to announce that Spring Batch 4.2.8 and 4.3.4 have been released and are available now from Maven Central repository. These patch releases come with a number of bug fixes and dependency updates. You can find the complete change logs here: [4.2.8](https://github.com/spring-projects/spring-batch/releases/tag/4.2.8.RELEASE) and [4.3.4](https://github.com/spring-projects/spring-batch/releases/tag/4.3.4).

I would like to thank all contributors who had a role in these releases!
