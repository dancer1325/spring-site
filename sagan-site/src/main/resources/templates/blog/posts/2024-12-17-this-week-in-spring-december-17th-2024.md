# [](#this-week-in-spring---december-17th-2024)This Week in Spring - December 17th, 2024

Hi, Spring fans! Welcome to another installment of a *Bootiful Podcast*! It's the 17th of December, 2024! And you know what means? The end of the year is nearly upon us! I can't believe it. It's been a *very* long year indeed, but I'm happy to get on board a flight bound for San Francisco soon and wind down my year in the company of friends and family. I hope you will too. Let's dive right into this week's roundup!

-   [Spring Data 2024.1.1 and 2024.0.7 released](https://spring.io/blog/2024/12/13/spring-data-2024)
-   the amazing [K. Siva Prasad Reddy (SivaLabs) on *A Bootiful Podcast*](https://spring.io/blog/2024/12/12/a-bootiful-podcast-sivalabs)
-   [Spring Framework 6.1.16 and 6.2.1 are available now](https://spring.io/blog/2024/12/12/spring-framework-6-1-16-and-6-2-1-available-now)
-   Announcing Spring AI MCP: [a Java SDK for the Model Context Protocol](https://spring.io/blog/2024/12/11/spring-ai-mcp-announcement)
-   [Introducing Spring AI Amazon Bedrock Nova Integratino via Converse API](https://spring.io/blog/2024/12/10/spring-ai-amazon-bedrock-nova)
-   A nice blog by Michael Minella: [Spring Support Updates](https://spring.io/blog/2024/12/10/spring-support-updates)
-   this is an interesting comparison of performance and efficiency in a Spring application implemented using the reactive system and [a Spring application implemented without reactive, and - importantly - without Java 21's virtual threads](https://www.dhaval-shah.com/parallel-processing-reactor-vs-jdk/) The author signalled to me on Twitter that he'd have a followup post looking at virtual threads, and I am quite keen on seeing that!
-   Not strictly speaking related to Spring, but very interesting: this shows how to use Testcontainers and Docker Compose, both of which live at Docker (the company), [together as one](https://mydeveloperplanet.com/2024/09/11/unit-integration-testing-with-testcontainers-docker-compose/)
-   two reasons you may want to disable the [Open Session in View filter in a Spring application](https://robertniestroj.hashnode.dev/two-reasons-why-you-might-want-to-disable-open-session-in-view-in-a-spring-application) (I think this is a good idea!)
-   Nice! A micro frontends [sample implementation](https://github.com/heerens/tractor-store-htmx-tailwind) of The Tractor Store built with Spring Boot + JTE, ESI, HTMX and Tailwind.
