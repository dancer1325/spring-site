# Spring Framework 5.2.0.M2, 5.1.7, 5.0.14 and 4.3.24 available now

_Releases | Stéphane Nicoll |  May 10, 2019 | 1 Comment_

On behalf of the team and everyone who has contributed, I am pleased to announce that Spring Framework `5.2.0.M2`, `5.1.7`, `5.0.14` and `4.3.24` are available from [repo.spring.io](https://repo.spring.io/release) as well as Maven Central now.

First things first, the second milestone of Spring Framework 5.2 includes [over 50 fixes and improvements](https://github.com/spring-projects/spring-framework/releases/tag/v5.2.0.M2) with improved RSocket integration and initial support for reactive transactions (more on that in a follow-up blog post soon). Spring Framework `5.1.7` includes [43 fixes and improvements](https://github.com/spring-projects/spring-framework/releases/tag/v5.1.7.RELEASE) and is a recommended upgrade for all `5.x` users.

Finally, we've also released Spring Framework [`5.0.14`](https://github.com/spring-projects/spring-framework/releases/tag/v5.0.14.RELEASE) and [`4.3.24`](https://github.com/spring-projects/spring-framework/releases/tag/v4.3.24.RELEASE) with a few selected fixes.

Once additional releases of the portfolio are available, we will release the Spring Boot counterparts by mid next week, stay tuned!
