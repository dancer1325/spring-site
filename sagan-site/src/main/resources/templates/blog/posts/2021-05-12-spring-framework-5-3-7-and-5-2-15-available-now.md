# Spring Framework 5.3.7 and 5.2.15 available now

_Releases | Stéphane Nicoll |  May 12, 2021 | 1 Comment_

**Update 2021-05-25:** The releases contains fixes for Spring Framework [CVE-2021-22118](https://tanzu.vmware.com/security/cve-2021-22118).

On behalf of the team and everyone who has contributed, I am pleased to announce that Spring Framework `5.3.7` and `5.2.15` are available now.

Spring Framework `5.3.7` includes [45 fixes and improvements](https://github.com/spring-projects/spring-framework/releases/tag/v5.3.7). Spring Framework `5.2.15` includes [9 selected fixes and improvements](https://github.com/spring-projects/spring-framework/releases/tag/v5.2.15.RELEASE).

Stay tuned for follow-up Spring Boot releases later this week.
