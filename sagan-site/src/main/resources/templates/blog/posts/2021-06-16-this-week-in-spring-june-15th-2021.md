Hi, Spring fans! How're you doing? I'm silly stoked! This week saw the release of Spring Native 0.10.0 and it is *awesome*! You absolutely have to check this release out. I put together an [episode of Spring Tips](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=JsUAGJqdvaA), the most comprehensive of its kind, introducing (almost) everything you'll need to know about Spring Native and Spring Native 0.10.0 in particular. Start here. Spring Native is awesome and this is the comprehensive introduction I wish I'd had at the start.

Farewell (for now) to Spring, the season, and long live Spring, the easiest and most productive path to production for your applications.
