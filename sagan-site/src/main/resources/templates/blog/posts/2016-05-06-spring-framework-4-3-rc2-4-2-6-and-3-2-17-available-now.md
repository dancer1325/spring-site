# Spring Framework 4.3 RC2, 4.2.6 and 3.2.17 available now

_Releases | Stéphane Nicoll |  May 06, 2016 | 2 Comments_

It is my pleasure to announce that the Spring Framework [4.2.6](https://jira.spring.io/secure/ReleaseNote.jspa?projectId=10000&version=15460) and [3.2.17](https://jira.spring.io/secure/ReleaseNote.jspa?projectId=10000&version=15518) maintenance releases [are available now](http://projects.spring.io/spring-framework). We also released [the second release candidate of the 4.3 line](https://jira.spring.io/secure/ReleaseNote.jspa?projectId=10000&version=15487).

4.3 RC2 contains many refinements and fixes, especially in the web framework area. We are about to finalize 4.3 with a scheduled GA in early June. Preparations towards merging 5.0 features into master will happen right afterwards, with a first 5.0 milestone expected in July.
