# This Week in Spring - November 15th, 2016

_Engineering | Josh Long |  November 15, 2016 | 0 Comments_

Welcome to another installment of *This Week in Spring*! Time sure is flying! We've got so much to get into this week. Can you believe we're staring down 2017? We've got so much to cover so let's get to it!

-   I hope you'll join Axon-framework lead Allard Buijze and I tomorrow when we introduce [how to build CQRS-centric applications with the Axon Framework and Spring Boot](https://spring.io/blog/2016/10/25/webinar-bootiful-cqrs-with-axon-nov-16)
-   More SpringOne Platform 2016 talks are available, and if you're interested in how Spring and reactive programming are coming together you'll want to hear directly from Rossen about [where the team is starting it's journey!](https://spring.io/blog/2016/11/15/springone-platform-2016-replay-from-imperative-to-reactive-web-apps)
