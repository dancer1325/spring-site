# This Week in Spring - June 12th, 2018

_Engineering | Josh Long |  June 13, 2018 | 1 Comment_

Hi Spring fans and welcome to another installment of *This Week in Spring*! This week I'm in NYC for the [SpringOne Tour NYC event](https://springonetour.io/2018/nyc), then it's off to Amsterdam for some customer visits. It's been a wild week in Spring so let's get to it!

-   Hi Spring fans! In this installment of *Spring Tips* we [look at the Java Persistence API](https://spring.io/blog/2018/06/06/spring-tips-jpa)
-   Don't miss this webinar on [MongoDB by Spring Data's Christoph Strobl and Mat Keep](https://content.pivotal.io/webinars/jun-20-next-generation-spring-data-and-mongodb-webinar)
-   [Spring Fu](https://github.com/spring-projects/spring-fu) is here and it's *awesome*! Just check out this example application of the new Kotlin-first [microframework](https://github.com/spring-projects/spring-fu/blob/master/samples/reactive-webapp/src/main/kotlin/org/springframework/fu/sample/reactive/Application.kt) for Spring developers. Spring Fu is explicit, not implicit. It's functional, it's fast, it's Spring Fu.
