# Spring LDAP 2.2 RC1

_Releases | Rob Winch |  October 04, 2016 | 1 Comment_

On behalf of the community, I am pleased to announce the release of Spring LDAP 2.2.0.RC1.

# [](#whats-new-in-spring-ldap-22)[](#what-s-new-in-spring-ldap-2-2)What’s New in Spring LDAP 2.2

You can find highlights of what’s new in the [What’s New in Spring LDAP 2.2](http://docs.spring.io/spring-ldap/docs/2.2.0.RC1/reference/#what-s-new-in-2-2) section of the reference.

# [](#contributions)[](#contributions)Contributions

Without the community we couldn’t be the successful project we are today. I’d like to thank everyone that created issues & provided feedback.

You can also ping me [@rob\_winch](https://twitter.com/rob_winch) or Joe [@joe\_grandja](https://twitter.com/joe_grandja) on Twitter.
