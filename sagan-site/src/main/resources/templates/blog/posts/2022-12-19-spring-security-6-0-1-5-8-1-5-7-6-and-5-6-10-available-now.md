# Spring Security 6.0.1, 5.8.1, 5.7.6 and 5.6.10 available now

_Releases | Marcus Hert Da Coregio |  December 19, 2022 | 0 Comments_

On behalf of the team and everyone who has contributed, I am pleased to announce that Spring Security 6.0.1, 5.8.1, 5.7.6 and 5.6.10 are available now. In all cases, the releases are mostly composed of bug fixes and documentation improvements.

To learn more, please visit the [6.0.1](https://github.com/spring-projects/spring-security/releases/tag/6.0.1), [5.8.1](https://github.com/spring-projects/spring-security/releases/tag/5.8.1), [5.7.6](https://github.com/spring-projects/spring-security/releases/tag/5.7.6) and [5.6.10](https://github.com/spring-projects/spring-security/releases/tag/5.6.10) release summaries.
