# Spring Framework 6.2.15 Available Now

_Releases | Brian Clozel |  December 11, 2025 | 0 Comments_

On behalf of the team and everyone who has contributed, I am pleased to announce that Spring Framework `6.2.15` is available now.

Spring Framework `6.2.15` ships with [31 fixes and documentation improvements](https://github.com/spring-projects/spring-framework/releases/tag/v6.2.15). This version will be shipped next week with Spring Boot `3.4.13` and `3.5.9`.
