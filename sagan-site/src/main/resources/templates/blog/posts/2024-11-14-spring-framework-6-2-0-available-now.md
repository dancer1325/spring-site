# Spring Framework 6.2.0 Available Now

_Releases | Brian Clozel |  November 14, 2024 | 1 Comment_

On behalf of the team and everyone who has contributed, I am pleased to announce that Spring Framework `6.2.0` is available now. We would like to thank our community of "early adopters" who helped us refine this version during the milestone phase.

This new Spring generation will materialize in Spring Boot applications [next week with Spring Boot 3.4.0](https://github.com/spring-projects/spring-boot/milestone/353). We wrote [extensive release notes](https://github.com/spring-projects/spring-framework/wiki/Spring-Framework-6.2-Release-Notes) that will let you know how to easily upgrade and leverage the new features.

The Framework team is already working on [the next major release as announced previously](https://spring.io/blog/2024/10/01/from-spring-framework-6-2-to-7-0). Keep an eye on our [official support timeline](https://spring.io/projects/spring-framework#support) to better schedule your future upgrades.
