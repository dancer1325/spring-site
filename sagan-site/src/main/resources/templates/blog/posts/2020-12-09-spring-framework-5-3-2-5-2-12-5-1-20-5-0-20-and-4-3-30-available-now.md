# Spring Framework 5.3.2, 5.2.12, 5.1.20, 5.0.20, and 4.3.30 available now

_Releases | Stéphane Nicoll |  December 09, 2020 | 4 Comments_

On behalf of the team and everyone who has contributed, I am pleased to announce a full round of Spring Framework releases.

Spring Framework 5.3.2 includes [54 fixes and improvements](https://github.com/spring-projects/spring-framework/releases/tag/v5.3.2). Spring Framework 5.2.12 includes [18 fixes and improvements](https://github.com/spring-projects/spring-framework/releases/tag/v5.2.12.RELEASE). Spring Framework 5.1.20 includes [14 selected fixes and improvements](https://github.com/spring-projects/spring-framework/releases/tag/v5.1.20.RELEASE).

The last maintenance releases for `5.0.x` (`5.0.20`) and `4.3.x` (`4.3.30`) with [12 selected fixes and improvements](https://github.com/spring-projects/spring-framework/releases/tag/v5.0.20.RELEASE) and [8 selected fixes and improvements](https://github.com/spring-projects/spring-framework/releases/tag/v4.3.20.RELEASE) respectively are also available today.

Stay tuned for follow-up Spring Boot releases 2.2.12, 2.3.7, and 2.4.1!
