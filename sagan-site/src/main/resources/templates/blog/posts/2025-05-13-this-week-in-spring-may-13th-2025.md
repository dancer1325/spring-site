# This Week in Spring - May 13th, 2025

_Engineering | Josh Long |  May 13, 2025 | 0 Comments_

Hi, Spring fans! As I write this, I'm at the amazing Code Remix event in Miami (well, technically Tampa), Florida. I'll also be speaking at the Tampa JUG while I'm there, so look out! After that, I'll be headed back to Europe—a wee bit further north this time—to Stockholm for the amazing JForum event, where I'll be doing a much deeper dive into all things Spring.

You know what I'm most excited about? You don't have to look far to figure it out—just check out the ol' [Spring Calendar](https://calendar.spring.io). Spring AI releases on May 20th! And then, a few short and sweet days later—which, by the way, I'll be spending in beautiful Barcelona, Spain at Spring I/O—out comes Spring Boot 3.5! Let's goooo!

Speaking of Spring I/O, I'll be there along with Spring legends Rod Johnson and Juergen Hoeller, co-founders of the project more than 20 years ago, doing a talk called *From Beans to Boot, Aspects to AI*. Do *NOT* miss this!

I'll also be doing a presentation with GraalVM founder Thomas Wuerthinger.

Spring Boot is a big release, but we've already got plenty of exciting GA versions of Spring Boot. Spring AI, on the other hand? Not so much. This is a *big* deal—*years* in the making! (Listen, if anybody could've predicted the rate of change in the AI space when we first started, I bet we would've released things a little differently…)

Anyway, my friends, we've got a ton of things to look at this week, so let's dive right in!

-   I really liked Sébastien Deleuze's (French-language) talk on optimizing Spring applications [from Devoxx FR 2025](https://www.youtube.com/watch?si=J_xjHb1AhC8t2e9j&v=4EAxhhSWgw4&feature=youtu.be&themeRefresh=1).
-   I also loved his talk [on null safety with JSpecify and NullAway](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=F_Qyo1cCpvY). One thing that struck me about this second talk is that YouTube's audio dubbing feature kicked in and translated the video into this weird English audio (even though I also speak French). Neat! So maybe you can watch both of them in whatever language you're most comfortable with.
-   [Spring gRPC has been promoted to a top-level project](https://spring.io/blog/2025/05/13/spring-grpc-promoted).
-   In last week's installment of *A Bootiful Podcast*, I talked to security legend [V Körbes](https://spring.io/blog/2025/05/08/a-bootiful-podcast-v-korbes).
-   Soon, there'll be a Spring AI RC1 release. Get the bits as soon as that happens and let us know! We're racing toward a Spring AI 1.0 release on the [20th of May](https://calendar.spring.io), and we want to hear from you if there are any show-stopper bugs!
-   The MongoDB team has updated their guidance [on using Spring Boot and MongoDB – check it out!](https://www.mongodb.com/resources/products/compatibilities/spring-boot)
-   A nice little look [at the Spring IoC (inversion of control) container](https://medium.com/@a.kago1988/understanding-spring-boots-ioc-container-43bc8e7530ac)
-   The Kotlin language page on using Spring Boot has been updated, too! [Check it out here.](https://kotlinlang.org/docs/jvm-create-project-with-spring-boot.html)
-   Want to learn about Spring AI ahead of the 1.0 release? [Who better than Dr. Mark Pollack, founder of Spring AI, and me – his trusty sidekick – to help?](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=uPWebxgEwlE&t=12s) Watch this!
