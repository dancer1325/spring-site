# This Week in Spring  - March 5th, 2019

_Engineering | Josh Long |  March 05, 2019 | 0 Comments_

Hi Spring fans! Welcome to another installment of *This Week in Spring*! I flew 14 hours to Tel Aviv, Israel. I then spent 28h on the ground visiting customers, visiting [my friends at Codota](https://twitter.com/starbuxman/status/1103056979201806337), and then presenting at a packed house at [the Israel Java User Group](https://twitter.com/odedia/status/1102610921908781056). Then I flew another 14 hours back to San Francisco, CA where I presented for a four-hour online course on using Kotlin with Spring Boot. Tonight I'll fly to Atlanta, GA for the incredible DevNexus conference where I'll be presenting (twice) with the [good Dr. Venkat Subramaniam](https://devnexus.com/presentations/3545/) on Kotlin (and Spring) and more. Tomorrow, I'm doing [a four hour course on testing. Join me!](https://www.safaribooksonline.com/live-training/courses/bootiful-testing/0636920239550/)
