# A Bootiful Podcast: JFrog's Baruch Sadogursky on continuous delivery, DevOps, Java artifact management and more

_Engineering | Josh Long |  August 07, 2020 | 1 Comment_

Hi, Spring fans! In this installment [Josh Long (@starbuxman)](http://twitter.com/starbuxman) talks about ten years on the Spring team, reactive support, and RSocket support in Spring Integration, his upcoming appearances at SpringOne and his [upcoming YOW! workshop on *Cloud Native Java*](https://www.eventbrite.com.au/e/yow-workshop-2020-cloud-native-java-aug-14-18-2020-tickets-114222161560?aff=social), and then talks to JFrog's [Baruch Sadogursky (@jbaruch)](http://twitter.com/jbaruch) about continuous delivery, DevOps, Java artifact management Artifactory, and more.
