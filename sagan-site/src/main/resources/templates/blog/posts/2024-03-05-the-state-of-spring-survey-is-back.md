# The State of Spring Survey Is Back!

_News | Michael Minella |  March 05, 2024 | 0 Comments_

We are excited to announce the State of Spring Survey! Make sure your voice is heard by heading over to [https://spring.io/survey](https://spring.io/survey "State of Spring Survey") and give us your feedback!

---

Are you excited to use virtual threads? Is AI all hype or is it game changing? What would you change about Spring if you could? ([You can](https://github.com/spring-projects/spring-boot/blob/main/CONTRIBUTING.adoc), by the way.)

The State of Spring survey is back! Last time we learned that 90% of respondents were looking forward to virtual threads through Project Loom. We learned that building APIs was still the main use case for Spring applications, with plain JSON over HTTP as the number one type of API. We also learned that native compilation with GraalVM was beginning to cool off a bit from its high point in popularity. What will we learn this year?

We are excited to hear from you, our community, on what matters in your use of Spring. Completing the survey should take about 15 minutes. If you want to be among the first to get the report, feel free to leave your email at the end of the survey.

Finally, we would like to ask for your help. We want to be sure as many members of the community as possible are heard. Please share the link [https://spring.io/survey](https://spring.io/survey "State of Spring Survey") or our [Twitter/X](https://twitter.com/springcentral/status/1765075456674631831) and [LinkedIn](https://www.linkedin.com/feed/update/urn:li:activity:7170842814371602434?utm_source=share&utm_medium=member_desktop) posts.

Thank you!

The Spring Team
