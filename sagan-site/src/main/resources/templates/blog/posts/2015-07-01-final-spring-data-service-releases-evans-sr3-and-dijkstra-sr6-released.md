# Final Spring Data Service Releases Evans SR3 and Dijkstra SR6 released

_Releases | Oliver Drotbohm |  July 01, 2015 | 3 Comments_

On behalf of the Spring Data team I'd like to announce the availability of the final service release of the Spring Data release trains Evans and Dijkstra. Evans SR3 ships [114 tickets fixed](https://jira.spring.io/issues/?filter=14961), Dijkstra SR6 has [56 bugs squashed](https://jira.spring.io/issues/?filter=15031).

Dijkstra is the last release train compatible with Spring 3.2 and picks up the [latest service release](https://spring.io/blog/2015/06/30/spring-framework-4-1-7-3-2-14-released) of that as well. Evans has been superseded by Fowler (currently in use in Spring Boot 1.2) which saw a [first service release](https://spring.io/blog/2015/07/01/spring-data-fowler-sr-1-released) today as well. The service releases are basically considered a conclusion of the branches and upgrading to Fowler is recommended to all users.
