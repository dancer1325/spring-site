# Spring Security 5.7.1, 5.6.5, 5.5.8 Released

_Releases | Marcus Hert Da Coregio |  May 18, 2022 | 0 Comments_

A new round of releases for Spring Security is available now. The new releases fix a bug where the `StrictHttpFirewall` incorrectly rejects valid CJKV characters.

Please take a look at the [release notes](https://github.com/spring-projects/spring-security/releases) for more information.
