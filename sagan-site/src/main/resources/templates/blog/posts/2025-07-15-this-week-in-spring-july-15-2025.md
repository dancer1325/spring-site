# This Week in Spring - July 15th, 2025

_Engineering | Josh Long |  July 15, 2025 | 3 Comments_

Hi, Spring fans! It's already the 15th of July! We're closer to 2026 than we are to 2024. And time's sure flying. Like I will, tomorrow. I'll be flying to Denver for the amazing UBERCONF software show! I'll be doing a workshop and two talks, and if you're there, I hope you'll come say "hi"!

Let's get into this week's roundup, because it's a doozy!

-   this is technically from more than a week ago, but [Spring gRPC 0.9.0](https://spring.io/blog/2025/07/04/spring-grpc-0-9-0-available-now) is available now!
-   In last week's *A Bootiful Podcast*, I talked to Spring team alumnus and [API oracle Arjen Poutsma](https://spring.io/blog/2025/07/10/a-bootiful-podcast-arjen-poutsma)
-   [Spring Cloud 2024.0.2 (aka Moorgate) has been released](https://spring.io/blog/2025/07/14/spring-cloud-2024-0-2-aka-moorgate-has-been-released)
-   An interesting article with a bold claim: Cron Jobs Are Dead: [How I Used Temporal to Future-Proof My Spring Boot App](https://medium.com/@kanhaaggarwal/cron-jobs-are-dead-how-i-used-temporal-to-future-proof-my-spring-boot-app-fd36117875f4). I haven't played too much with Temporal, but it looks like its solving an interesting problem - workflow orchestration - in an interesting way, and offers a good Spring Boot integration.
-   I did a talk for Jetbrains with IntelliJ IDEA lead Aleksey Stukalov looking at a [lot of the amazing new features in IntelliJ IDEA](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=CsR7pFFhmIM)
-   did you see Spring Boot lead Phil Webb's amazing talk, [lessons from 12 years of Spring Boot](https://youtube.com/watch?v=rJcQqBGEJw4&si=r499UdHjMg1AsVKw)
-   This is a nice look at the new [Spring Boot 4 release (coming in November!)](https://blog.stackademic.com/spring-boot-4-released-a-full-analysis-of-11-major-changes-c98bda2bb358)
-   the amazing `datasource-proxy` project - which makes it trivial to create adapters around JDBC code - [has a new release!](1.11.0) .
-   *Eeenteresting*! The CEO of Akka, the new name for the company formerly known as Lightbend, which is the new name for the company formerly known as Typesafe, has just announced a [new *agentic* AI framework](https://www.linkedin.com/feed/update/urn:li:activity:7350256382626975745) and its integration with Spring Boot.
-   *Finally*! Tired of those long Maven builds, especially when doing GraalVM native image compilations? Want some good music? Well, I [certainly do](https://github.com/oracle/graal/issues/5327)! And the community did not disappoint! Richard Fichtner developed the [Music Maven Plugin, which plays music while your build is running](https://bsky.app/profile/richard.fichtner.dev/post/3lfmusrdxor2i)! Amazing. But wouldn't it be great if it could also play music from Spotify? Mohammed Aboullaite, who works at Spotify, sent a PR! [And now it can](https://github.com/xdev-software/music-maven-plugin?tab=readme-ov-file#spotify)!
