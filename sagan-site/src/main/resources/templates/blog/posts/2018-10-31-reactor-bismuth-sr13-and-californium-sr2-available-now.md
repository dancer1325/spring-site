# Reactor Bismuth-SR13 and Californium-SR2 available now !

_Engineering | Stephane Maldini |  October 31, 2018 | 0 Comments_

# [](#californium-update)Californium Update

On behalf of the Reactor team, I am pleased to announce that `Californium-SR2` release train [is available now](http://projectreactor.io/docs).

This maintenance release of the *Californium* line includes the following dependencies updates:

-   Reactor-Core *3.2.2* | [Release Notes](https://github.com/reactor/reactor-core/releases/tag/v3.2.2.RELEASE) | [Repository](https://github.com/reactor/reactor-core)
-   Reactor-Netty *0.8.2* | [Release Notes](https://github.com/reactor/reactor-netty/releases/tag/v0.8.2.RELEASE) | [Repository](https://github.com/reactor/reactor-netty)

# [](#bismuth-update)Bismuth Update

We also have shipped `Bismuth-SR13`. This maintenance release of the *Bismuth* line includes the following dependencies updates:

-   Reactor-Core *3.1.11* | [Release Notes](https://github.com/reactor/reactor-core/releases/tag/v3.1.11.RELEASE) | [Repository](https://github.com/reactor/reactor-core/tree/3.1.x)
-   Reactor-Netty *0.7.11* | [Release Notes](https://github.com/reactor/reactor-netty/releases/tag/v0.7.11.RELEASE) | [Repository](https://github.com/reactor/reactor-netty/tree/0.7.x)

Thanks a lot to our contributors, and as usual, update while it's hot :)
