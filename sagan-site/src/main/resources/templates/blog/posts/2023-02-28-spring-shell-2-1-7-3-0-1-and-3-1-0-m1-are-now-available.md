# Spring Shell 2.1.7, 3.0.1 and 3.1.0-M1 are now available

_Releases | Janne Valkealahti |  February 28, 2023 | 0 Comments_

On behalf of the team and everyone who has contributed, I'm happy to announce that Spring Shell `2.1.7`, `3.0.1` and `3.1.0-M1` has been released and is now available from Maven Central and [https://repo.spring.io/milestone](https://repo.spring.io/milestone) respectively.

Please see the [release notes 2.1.7](https://github.com/spring-projects/spring-shell/releases/tag/v2.1.7), [release notes 3.0.1](https://github.com/spring-projects/spring-shell/releases/tag/v3.0.1) and [release notes 3.1.0-M1](https://github.com/spring-projects/spring-shell/releases/tag/v3.1.0-M1) for more details.

Thanks to all those who have contributed with issue reports and pull requests.
