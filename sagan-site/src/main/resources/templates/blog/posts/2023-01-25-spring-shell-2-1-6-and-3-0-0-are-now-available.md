# Spring Shell 2.1.6 and 3.0.0 are now available

_Releases | Janne Valkealahti |  January 25, 2023 | 0 Comments_

On behalf of the team and everyone who has contributed, I'm happy to announce that Spring Shell `2.1.6` and `3.0.0` has been released and is now available from Maven Central.

Please see the [release notes 2.1.6](https://github.com/spring-projects/spring-shell/releases/tag/v2.1.6) and [release notes 3.0.0](https://github.com/spring-projects/spring-shell/releases/tag/v3.0.0) for more details.

Thanks to all those who have contributed with issue reports and pull requests.
