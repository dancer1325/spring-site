# Spring Security 6.4.6 Released

_Releases | Josh Cummings |  May 19, 2025 | 0 Comments_

On behalf of the team and everyone who has contributed, I am pleased to announce the availability of Spring Security 6.4.6.

Please check [the changelog](https://github.com/spring-projects/spring-security/releases/tag/6.4.6) for more details. Note that this release contains the fix for [CVE-2025-41232](https://spring.io/security/cve-2025-41232).
