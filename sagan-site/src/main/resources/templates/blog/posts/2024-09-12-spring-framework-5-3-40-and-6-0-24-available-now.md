# Spring Framework 5.3.40 and 6.0.24 available now

_Releases | Brian Clozel |  September 12, 2024 | 11 Comments_

On behalf of the team, I am pleased to announce that Spring Framework 5.3.40 and 6.0.24 have been released for support customers. The releases are available from [https://packages.broadcom.com](https://packages.broadcom.com).

-   Spring Framework [5.3.40](https://enterprise.spring.io/projects/spring-framework/5.3.40) ships with 1 bug fix.
-   Spring Framework [6.0.24](https://enterprise.spring.io/projects/spring-framework/6.0.24) ships with 2 bug fixes.

Those are out of cycle releases that address [CVE-2024-38816](https://spring.io/security/cve-2024-38816), we will resume our usual 3 months cadence for 5.3.x and 6.0.x commercial releases.
