# This Week in Spring, May 15th, 2012

_Engineering | Josh Long |  May 16, 2012 | 0 Comments_

Welcome back to another installment of *This week in Spring!*. We've got a lot to cover this week, as usual. So, onward!

1.  [Rossen Stoyanchev](http://blog.springsource.org/author/rstoyanchev/) has just released part two and three of his blog series introducing Spring MVC 3.2's new features. In the first installment, Rossen introduces how to make a [Spring MVC `@Controller` asynchronous](http://blog.springsource.org/2012/05/10/spring-mvc-3-2-preview-making-a-controller-method-asynchronous). In the second post, Rossen introduces [how to add long polling to an existing web application](http://blog.springsource.org/2012/05/13/spring-mvc-3-2-preview-adding-long-polling-to-an-existing-web-application/). Long polling is useful in any number of scenarios where you want to simulate server-side push with client-side applications.
