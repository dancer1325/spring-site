# Spring Framework 5.2.4 and 5.1.14 available now

_Releases | Stéphane Nicoll |  February 25, 2020 | 1 Comment_

On behalf of the team and everyone who has contributed, I am pleased to announce that Spring Framework 5.2.4 and 5.1.14 are available now.

The fourth maintenance release of the 5.2 line includes [over 60 fixes and improvements](https://github.com/spring-projects/spring-framework/releases/tag/v5.2.4.RELEASE). Spring Framework 5.1.14 [includes 14 selected fixes and improvements](https://github.com/spring-projects/spring-framework/releases/tag/v5.1.14.RELEASE).

As usual, we'll follow up shortly with corresponding Spring Boot releases (2.2.5 and 2.1.13).
