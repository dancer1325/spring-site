# This Week in Spring - June 11th, 2024

_Engineering | Josh Long |  June 10, 2024 | 0 Comments_

# [](#this-week-in-spring---june-10th-2024)This Week in Spring - June 10th, 2024

Hi, Spring fans! Welcome to another installment of *This Week in Spring*! I'm in Paris, France, to talk to organizations using and working with Spring. Then, next week, it's off to Krakow, Poland, for the amazing Devoxx PL event! I can't wait. If you're around, don't hesitate to say "hi"!

We've got an amazing roundup, so let's dive right into it!

-   [I did a talk at Spring IO 2024 in Barcelona that introduced the latest-and-greatest in Spring Boot, and I think you might like it](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=2Aa5uQozbJQ). (Others did; it was voted the most popular talk...)
-   Two short weeks ago, I was in Barcelona, Spain, for the amazing Spring IO event where I did a talk and a keynote. The keynote, delivered with my colleague and friend Christian Tsolov, was a blazing fast introduction to all things Spring AI. Spring IO was nice enough [to lend me the footage, and it's presented here](https://spring.io/blog/2024/06/05/spring-tips-spring-ai-redux) for your enjoyment. Trust me, this is a *great* use of 20-ish minutes.
-   It was a lot of fun to talk to Bellsoft developer advocate Pasha Finkelshteyn in this episode of [*A Bootiful Podcast*](https://spring.io/blog/2024/06/06/a-bootiful-podcast-jetbrains-developer-advocate-pasha-finkelshteyn).
-   Speaking of Bellsoft, they have an amazing blog that looks [at Spring Boot 3.3's new support for CDS](https://bell-sw.com/blog/how-to-use-cds-with-spring-boot-applications/).
-   It was fun sitting down with my [friend Baruch Sagadoursky](https://x.com/gradle/status/1798731001172316395?s=12&t=n-UflcIbnx1lage-TBk0Cg) at Devoxx FR to talk about Gradle, Spring Boot, Spring AI, and more.
-   I liked this blog [on implementing caching in Spring Boot](https://medium.com/@sehgal.mohit06/implementing-caching-in-spring-boot-application-9b7cf2f55f8e).
-   Good news, everybody! [The GraalVM team is working on WebAssembly support and soliciting feedback](https://t.co/WgpidxxbNL).
-   [SpringOne 2024 is going virtual!](https://x.com/VMwareTanzu/status/1797691723491037467?s=12&t=n-UflcIbnx1lage-TBk0Cg)
-   From Netflix's Paul Bakker: "Reminder to switch your DGS starter dependency to leverage DGS/Spring GraphQL integration. This will be the new default soon, so please test with your apps. We've seen no issues switching over at Netflix 🙌" [See this for more](https://netflix.github.io/dgs/spring-graphql-integration/).
-   JetBrains Developer Advocate watched my talk and has some notes! Did you know [IntelliJ can automatically bring up the possible collaborating beans and let me inject them?](https://twitter.com/antonarhipov/status/1799793277606170956?s=61&t=wR6lIrIa5I0gNYlBX63f7g).
