# Video: SpringOne 2GX - Grails in the Cloud

_News | Adam Fitzgerald |  December 19, 2011 | 0 Comments_

[![SpringOne 2GX Video: Grails in the Cloud with Peter Ledbrook](http://www.springsource.org/files/SpringOne2GX2011GrailsInTheCloud.png)](http://www.infoq.com/presentations/Grails-in-the-Cloud)

Now that everyone has had a chance to see the [keynote](/node/3321) [presentations](/node/3322) from SpringOne 2GX 2011 and that [Grails 2.0 has been released](http://blog.springsource.org/2011/12/15/grails-2-0-released/) we can share more conference sessions from the 2GX side of the show. This video presentation is by [Peter Ledbrook](http://blog.springsource.org/author/peter-ledbrook/), Developer Advocate for Grails, and he discusses running [Grails in the Cloud](http://www.infoq.com/presentations/Grails-in-the-Cloud). He compares the different cloud providers out there and looks at some of the best solutions for hosting your Grails applications.

Many thanks to [InfoQ](http://www.infoq.com) for coming to Chicago to record so many of the fantastic [SpringOne 2GX presentations](http://www.infoq.com/springone_2gx_2011/).
