# Spring LDAP 3.1.4 and 3.2.2 are available

_Releases | Josh Cummings |  February 21, 2024 | 0 Comments_

On behalf of the community, I’m pleased to announce the release of Spring LDAP 3.1.4 and 3.2.2! These releases include dependency updates and minor bug fixes.

You can find the complete details in the [3.1.4](https://github.com/spring-projects/spring-ldap/releases/tag/3.1.4) and [3.2.2](https://github.com/spring-projects/spring-ldap/releases/tag/3.2.2) release notes.
