# This Week in Spring - May 9th, 2023

_Engineering | Josh Long |  May 09, 2023 | 0 Comments_

Hi, Spring fans! Welcome to another wonderful, fancyful installment of *This Week in Spring*! I was just at the first [Devoxx GR](https://devoxx.gr), in the sunny mediterranean city of Athens, Greece. Uh, yah, this was a good'un. If you can get to it, you should. Don't miss next year's installment if you missed this one. And now, I'm in London, for one of my all time favorite shows, Devoxx UK. Are you going to [Devoxx UK](https://devoxx.co.uk)? Come find me! I'll be talking about Spring Boot 3, and it should be fun. After this, I'm off to Germany where I *may* be appearing at a JUG near Wolfsburg ([I'll keep you posted](https://twitter.com/starbuxman)). Then, it's off [to Spring IO](https://2023.springio.net/) in beautiful Barcelona, which is sure to be a ton of fun. Seriously, if you get one show in western Europe, make it this one. And then, finally, it's off to [ITKonekt](https://itkonekt.com/en/) in Belgrade Friends, we've got a whole host of things to get to this week, so let's dive right into it!
