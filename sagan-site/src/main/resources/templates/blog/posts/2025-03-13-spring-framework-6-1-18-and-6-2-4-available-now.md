# Spring Framework 6.1.18 and 6.2.4 Available Now

_Releases | Brian Clozel |  March 13, 2025 | 0 Comments_

On behalf of the team and everyone who has contributed, I am pleased to announce that Spring Framework `6.1.18` and `6.2.4` are available now.

Spring Framework `6.1.18` ships with [11 fixes and documentation improvements](https://github.com/spring-projects/spring-framework/releases/tag/v6.1.18). This version will be shipped next week with Spring Boot `3.3.10`.

Spring Framework `6.2.4` ships with [46 fixes and documentation improvements](https://github.com/spring-projects/spring-framework/releases/tag/v6.2.4). This version will be shipped next week with Spring Boot `3.4.4` and `3.5.0-M3`.
