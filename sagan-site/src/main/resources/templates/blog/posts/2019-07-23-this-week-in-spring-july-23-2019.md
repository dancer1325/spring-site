# This Week in Spring - July 23, 2019

_Engineering | Josh Long |  July 23, 2019 | 1 Comment_

Hi Spring fans! Welcome to another installment of *This Week in Spring*! I concluded my time in Portland, OR, for OSCON on Sunday, went to Chicago for the epic Chicago, IL Cloud Conf yesterday; today I am in Denver, CO, for meetings; and then Wednesday I am off to Burlington, MA for the SpringOne Tour 2019 event. We've got some good stuff this week so let's get to it!

-   Hi Spring fans! In last week's episode of [*A Bootiful Podcast*, I talk to Azul CTO Gil Tene on Java, JVMs, garbage collection, and a ton more](https://spring.io/blog/2019/07/19/a-bootiful-podcast-azul-cto-gil-tene-on-java-jvms-garbage-collection-and-a-ton-more)
-   There's an interesting new feature on `master` for Spring Cloud Gateway giving [improved details about your routes in the Actuator](https://github.com/spring-cloud/spring-cloud-gateway/pull/785#issuecomment-513988787)
-   Check out how the Dutch government uses [Cloud Foundry to enhance users experience](https://www.cloudfoundry.org/blog/dutch-government-uses-cloud-foundry-enhance-user-experience/)
-   It's finally here! Check out the new support for the [Cloud Foundry PAS running on Kubernetes](https://content.pivotal.io/pivotal-announcements/pivotal-makes-kubernetes-easier-for-developers-and-operators?utm_campaign=pr-pivotal-makes-kubernetes-easier-q219&utm_source=twitter&utm_medium=social&utm_content=1562979455). Here's another post with details from our partners over at VMWare [on how PAS on Kubernetes improves the developer experience](https://blogs.vmware.com/cloudnative/2019/07/16/pas-on-kubernetes-improves-the-developer-experience/)
-   A nice post on the Okta blog on how to [monitor your Java applications with the Spring Boot Actuator](https://developer.okta.com/blog/2019/07/17/monitoring-with-actuator)
-   [Have you registered for the Burlington, MA installment of the SpringOne Tour](https://springonetour.io/2019/burlington)?
-   An interesting [video on creating REST APIs with Spring Boot](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=-wruptabpq4&feature=share)
-   InfoQ has a nice post on [the updates to the Spring Initializr tool](https://www.infoq.com/news/2019/07/spring-initializr-tool-updated/?itm_source=presentations_about_Spring-Boot&itm_medium=link&itm_campaign=Spring-Boot)
-   I liked this post by Andriy Redko on [testing Spring Boot `Conditional`s](https://dzone.com/articles/testing-spring-boot-conditionals-the-sane-way)
-   Check out Rajesh Bjojwani's look at the [feature called App Revisions in Pivotal Cloud Foundry 2.6](https://dzone.com/articles/pcf-26-feature-app-revisions)
