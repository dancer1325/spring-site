# This Week in Spring - February 4th, 2025

_Engineering | Josh Long |  February 04, 2025 | 0 Comments_

Hi, Spring fans! Welcome to another installment of *This Week in Spring*! It's February 4th, 2025, as I write this. We are ten days away from Valentine's day, and about a month away from Devnexus. Lots to look forward to, in both the short term and the long term!

Let's dive right into this week's roundup!

-   Thanks very much to the San Francisco JUG for letting Spring Boot co-founder Phil Webb and I take a little stroll down memory lane, and revisit at the [first 10 whole years of Spring Boot](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=SwKrrLCVTH0&t=26s). This was a ton of fun!
-   our friend, Vaadin's Marcus Hellberg, has a really crazy example of a Spring Boot and Vaadin application [that uses Spring AI MCP to update itself!](https://twitter.com/marcushellberg/status/1886678718200078609?s=12)
-   Bellsoft's Catherine Edelveis has a nice video [on Dockerizing your Spring Boot applications](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=dwVPvZAPaxQ)
-   do you ever want an exploding code minigame from within your IntelliJ IDEA? [there's a plugin for that!](https://t.co/QlWVTb5xUV)
-   [Spring Cloud Stream Applications 2024.0.1 and 2025.0.0 are now available](https://spring.io/blog/2025/02/02/spring-cloud-stream-applications-2024_0_1-and-2025_0_0-are-now-available)
-   in last week's installment of *A Bootiful Podcast*, I talked to HTMX creator Carson Gross.
-   and just this morning, I had another discussion - but this time intended for Youtube! - with Carson, [looking at how to actually weild HTMX and Spring Boot together](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=huLLh_xgcl4)
-   Spring Boot 3.4 came out in November and brought with it a ton of cool features! Did you see the roundups I did looking at the various portfolio projects, both above and below that release? [What's new in Spring AI M4](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=6fYjOTVTvOc) , [What's new in Spring Modulith 1.3](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=bZoF3TPNXEE), [What's new in Spring Batch 5.2](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=phbYTF6xAaY), [What's new in Spring Integration 6.4](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=gGyg3gU5iY0), [What's new in Spring Security 6.4](https://youtu.be/lPreCE97nEw?si=ATQZ4aTKeoI03wqH), [What's new in Spring Data 2024.1?](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=DUIpWkl_ixE), and finally [What's new in Spring Framework 6.2](https://youtu.be/t2NEM-Jw430?si=YgzbhEOUUn8mQ-ew)
