This one is named for Ada Lovelace, an English mathematician and writer, she was chiefly known for her work on Charles Babbage's proposed mechanical general-purpose computer, the Analytical Engine. Once you're done reading more into this hero and this self described "poetical scientist", you might also [read up on the new features our current Spring Data Lovelace release train](https://twitter.com/springdata/status/1049696283626299393?s=12)!
