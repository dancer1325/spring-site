# This Week in Spring - May 4th, 2021

_Engineering | Josh Long |  May 05, 2021 | 0 Comments_

Hi, Spring fans! Welcome to another installment of *This Week in Spring*! How're you doing? I hope you're doing well, and that the Fourth (of May) is with you.

Today may be the 4th, but yesterday was the third of May, and that marks the 20th anniversary of the `JdbcTemplate`, which I think is super cool! [Happy birthday, `JdbcTemplate`](https://twitter.com/starbuxman/status/1389497863009280000)! The `JdbcTemplate` was important to a [lot](https://twitter.com/NewpPaul/status/1389526918010228739) [of](https://twitter.com/microwatts/status/1389510296876855298) [people](https://twitter.com/naveenkumaran_a/status/1389743476607127553) [because](https://twitter.com/microwatts/status/1389510296876855298) it's one of those great little utilities that made it easy to justify pulling Spring into applications almost two decades ago. It also helped people learn a lot about some of the design patterns that Spring was trying to make commonplace.
