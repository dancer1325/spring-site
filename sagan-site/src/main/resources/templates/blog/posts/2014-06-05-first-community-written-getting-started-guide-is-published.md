# First community-written getting started guide is published

_Engineering | Greg L. Turnquist |  June 05, 2014 | 1 Comment_

Greetings Spring community,

Today we have just published the first getting started guide written and submitted by a community member: [Producing a SOAP web service](http://spring.io/guides/gs/producing-web-service).

[Maciej Walkowiak](https://github.com/maciejwalkowiak) crafted a guide that served his needs and decided to contribute to the community. It lined up with [expressed interest in such a guide](https://github.com/spring-guides/getting-started-guides/issues/1) from others. Maciej used [https://github.com/spring-guides/getting-started-guide](https://github.com/spring-guides/getting-started-guide) as his template and submitted a [pull request](https://github.com/spring-guides/getting-started-guides/pull/2) to it containing his crafted guide.

We were able to merge all of his commits into an [independent repository](https://github.com/spring-guides/gs-producing-web-service), apply some [editorial polish](https://github.com/spring-guides/gs-producing-web-service/commits/master), and after final review, get it published today.

Cheers!
