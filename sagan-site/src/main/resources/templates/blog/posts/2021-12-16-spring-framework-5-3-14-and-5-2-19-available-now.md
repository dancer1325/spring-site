# Spring Framework 5.3.14 and 5.2.19 available now

_Releases | Stéphane Nicoll |  December 16, 2021 | 0 Comments_

**Update Jan 5, 2022:** The releases include fixes for [CVE-2021-22060](https://tanzu.vmware.com/security/cve-2021-22060) whose official publication was deferred until today since many people take time off at the end of the year.

On behalf of the team and everyone who has contributed, I am pleased to announce that Spring Framework `5.3.14` and `5.2.19` are available now.

Spring Framework `5.3.14` includes [36 fixes and improvements](https://github.com/spring-projects/spring-framework/releases/tag/v5.3.14). Spring Framework `5.2.19` includes [11 selected fixes and improvements](https://github.com/spring-projects/spring-framework/releases/tag/v5.2.19.RELEASE).

Stay tuned for the announcement of Spring Framework `6.0.0-M1` later today!
