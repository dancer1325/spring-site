# This Week in Spring - February 16th, 2021

_Engineering | Josh Long |  February 16, 2021 | 0 Comments_

Hi, Spring fans! Welcome to another installment of *This Week in Spring*! This week is fixing to be a really fun one!

I'll be speaking at the [**Seattle JUG** tonight](https://www.meetup.com/seajug/events/276135388/). It's free, online, virtual, etc. Join us, won't you?

I'll also be speaking at the legendary [**Devnexus**](http://DevNexus.com) event this week. Do *not* miss that. Or the show. It's free, being held virtually, online, and a *ton of us* from the Spring team will be there.
