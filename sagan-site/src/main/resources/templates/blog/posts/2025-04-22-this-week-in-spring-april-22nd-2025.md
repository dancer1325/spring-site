# This Week in Spring - April 22nd, 2025

_Engineering | Josh Long |  April 22, 2025 | 0 Comments_

Hi, Spring fans! Welcome to another installment of *This Week in Spring*, which I'm writing from magnificent Minneapolis, Minnesota, where I'm recording an amazing Frontend Masters course introducing Spring Boot.

-   I love this article introducing [Spring AI in JavaPro magazine](https://javapro.io/2025/04/22/building-ai-driven-applications-with-spring-ai/)
-   Want to run an LLM entirely in Java, supercharged with GraalVM native images? Check out this tweet from GraalVM developer advocate Alina Yurenko — [it's dope](https://x.com/alina_yurenko/status/1912478280948576356).
-   I loved it so much I made [a quick YouTube Short on the same topic](https://www.youtube.com/shorts/7zSEa_B-TEU).
-   By the way, want to learn Java? [This is a great resource](https://learn.java).
-   The amazing Spring AI co-lead Christian Tsolov has a [great talk introducing MCP](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=oSGVQIZxi7s).
-   Spring team alumnus Arjen Poutsma has helped shape the APIs in Spring for a long time with his famous [*Poutsma Principles* — in this blog, he discusses them](https://poutsma-principles.com/blog/2025/04/15/poutsma-princple/).
