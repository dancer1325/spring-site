# Spring Framework 6.0.0-M2 and 5.3.15 available now

_Releases | Stéphane Nicoll |  January 13, 2022 | 1 Comment_

On behalf of the team and everyone who has contributed, I am pleased to announce that Spring Framework `6.0.0-M2` and `5.3.15` are available now.

Spring Framework `5.3.15` includes [17 fixes and improvements](https://github.com/spring-projects/spring-framework/releases/tag/v5.3.15) as a recommended upgrade for all Spring production scenarios. Spring Framework `6.0.0-M2` includes [6 additional fixes and improvements](https://github.com/spring-projects/spring-framework/releases/tag/v6.0.0-M2) specific to the 6.0 branch.

Stay tuned for the announcement of Spring Boot `3.0.0-M1` next week!
