# Video: An Introduction To Spring Social

_News | Adam Fitzgerald |  October 04, 2011 | 0 Comments_

[Spring Social](/spring-social) is an extension of the Spring Framework that enables applications to connect with Software-as-a-Service (SaaS) providers such as Facebook and Twitter and interact with those services on behalf of their users. This presentations by [Craig Walls](http://blog.springsource.com/author/cwalls/) introduces Spring Social essentials such as connecting to service providers and using Spring Social's API bindings. Craig also describes how to add provider-based sign in and how to extend Spring Social's service provider support.
