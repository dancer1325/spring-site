# This Week in Spring - November 27th, 2018

_Engineering | Josh Long |  November 27, 2018 | 3 Comments_

Hi Spring fans! Welcome to another installment of *This Week in Spring!* This week I'm in Marikesh, Morocco for the epic [Devoxx MA](http://devoxx.ma) talking about all things Spring and cloud with my friends - Google's Ray Tsang and Microsoft's Bruno Borges. Then, tomorrow it's off to [Bern](https://www.jug.ch/html/events/2018/cloud_native_java_be.html) and [Zurich](https://www.jug.ch/html/events/2018/cloud_native_java.html) Switzerland \[for the local JUGs there. Tonight, in about 15 minutes, I'm doing a live training on Safari - ["Bootiful Kotlin,"](https://www.safaribooksonline.com/live-training/courses/spring-boot-and-kotlin/0636920221562/) too.
