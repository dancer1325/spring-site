# This Week in Spring - January 12th, 2021

_Engineering | Josh Long |  January 13, 2021 | 0 Comments_

I almost wrote in 2020! There's been so much going on I almost forgot to take stock of the *year*. What a rush. We better get to the roundup before I somehow lose even more track of time. So, without further ado, this week's roundup.

-   [Spring Framework 5.3.3 is now available](https://spring.io/blog/2021/01/11/spring-framework-5-3-3-available-now)
-   I posted another installment of *You May Not Need a Library For That*, this one looking at [the fabulous `WebClient`](https://spring.io/blog/2021/01/11/ymnnalft-http-clients)
-   [Spring Data's Migration from Jira to GitHub Issues](https://spring.io/blog/2021/01/07/spring-data-s-migration-from-jira-to-github-issues)
-   In last week's installment of a [Bootiful Podcast, I talked to Netty founder Trustin Lee](https://spring.io/blog/2021/01/07/a-bootiful-podcast-netty-and-armeria-founder-trustin-lee)
-   In last week's installment of *You May Not Need a Library For That*, I looked at [reactive dataflow with project Reactor](https://spring.io/blog/2021/01/06/ymnnalft-reactive-dataflow-with-project-reactor)
-   [A useful sheet full of information about GraalVM](https://twitter.com/shelajev/status/1347165207068610561?s=12)
-   [Oliver Drotbohm's collection of tiny helpers for building Spring applications](https://github.com/odrotbohm/spring-playground)
-   This is very cool: Ruchi Saini wrote a pretty long introduction on how to build [microservices with Spring (Cloud)](https://dzone.com/articles/how-to-create-microservices-using-spring)
-   Ram N. has published an interesting article looking at [Spring Boot CRUD operations with exception handling](https://dzone.com/articles/spring-boot-crud-operations-example-with-exception): nice!
-   Ram N. also posted a video looking [at Spring's `byName` auto-wiring mode](https://dzone.com/articles/what-is-byname-auto-wiring-mode-in-spring).
