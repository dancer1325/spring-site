# Spring HATEOAS 2.2.3 and 2.3.1 released

_Releases | Oliver Drotbohm |  July 12, 2024 | 0 Comments_

I am pleased to announce the releases of Spring HATEOAS 2.2.3 and 2.3.1. They both include an improved parser for [RFC-8288](https://httpwg.org/specs/rfc8288.html) Link header expressions as well as the ususal dependency upgrades.

Find more information in the full change log for [2.2.3](https://github.com/spring-projects/spring-hateoas/releases/tag/2.2.3) and [2.3.1](https://github.com/spring-projects/spring-hateoas/releases/tag/2.3.1).
