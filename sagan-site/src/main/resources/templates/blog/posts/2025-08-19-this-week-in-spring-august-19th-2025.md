# This Week in Spring - August 19th, 2025

_Engineering | Josh Long |  August 19, 2025 | 0 Comments_

Hi, Spring fans! Welcome to another extra special installment of *This Week in Spring* - special because the next installment will be delivered from the floors of the Ventian where the extraordinairily awesome [**SpringOne 2025**](https://springone.io) event will take place!

So, some poetry:

*T’was the Week Before SpringOne*

*T’was the week before SpringOne, when all through the code,* *Not a bean was left hanging, not even in load.* *The sessions were queued in the schedule with care,* *In hopes that the keynote soon would be there.*

*Developers nestled all snug at their keys,* *Dreaming of boot apps and APIs with ease.* *With visions of cloud-native dreams in their head,* *They packed for Las Vegas, excitement widespread.*

*For SpringOne was coming — bright lights and pure bliss —* *At the grand Venetian, none dared to miss.* *The premier of Spring, of Java, of cloud,* *A gathering epic, electric, and proud!*

I am *so* excited! And behind. There's oh-so-much to do and so little time in which to do it! Will [you be there](https://springone.io)? Be sure to say "hi"!
