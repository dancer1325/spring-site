# The Spring team at Devnexus 2023

_News | Josh Long |  April 04, 2023 | 0 Comments_

The Spring team is out in force here at Devnexus! I hope you'll come see us. Here's my annotated look at what sessions I want to see over the oh-so-brief three days of the show.

## [](#tuesday-april-4th-2023)Tuesday, April 4th, 2023

The fun starts today, with the [Spring Boot 3 workshop](https://devnexus.com/presentations/spring-boot-3-workshop) with Spring Boot cofounder Phil Webb and Micrometer and observability ninja Jonatan Ivanov

## [](#wednesday-april-5th-2023)Wednesday, April 5th, 2023

Tomorrow, the fun continues! At 10am, our friends over at Vaadin will have a talk, [*Full Stack Reactive with Spring Boot and React*](https://devnexus.com/presentations/full-stack-reactive-with-spring-boot-react), that looks mighty interesting! My friends and Nate Schutta and Dan Vega have a talk, [*Spring Recipes: A Collection of Common-Sense Solutions*](https://devnexus.com/presentations/spring-recipes-a-collection-of-common-sense-solutions), that I think will be super insightful.

At 11:30am, our friend Brian Sam-Bodden over at Redis will have a very interesting talk, [Vector Similiarity Search in Spring with Redis Stack](https://devnexus.com/presentations/vector-similarity-search-in-spring-with-redis-stack). My friend, the legendary, Mark Heckler, from Microsoft, will be giving a talk [*Let's Get Functional: the fast path to smart serverless with Spring Cloud Function and Azure Functions*](https://devnexus.com/presentations/let-s-get-functional-the-fast-path-to-smart-serverless-with-spring-cloud-function-azure-functions). Spring Batch community legend, and fellow Java Champion, Rodrigo Graciano will be doing a presentation [*Scaling Batch Applications*](https://devnexus.com/presentations/scaling-batch-applications).

At 1:30pm, Jared Rhodes - a Microsoft MVP and consultant at Epam Solutions - wil be doing an very interesting talk, [*Getting Started with Azure Spring Apps*](https://devnexus.com/presentations/getting-started-with-azure-spring-apps). While not strictly speaking related to Spring, our friends over at Gradle, Steve Poole and Justin Reock, have an interesting talk about developer productivity called [*Developer Productivity - clean | secure | fast*](https://devnexus.com/presentations/developer-productivity-clean-secure-fast).

The fun continues at 3pm, with Glen Renfro and Felipe Gutierrez giving a talk on the new-ish [Spring Modulith](https://devnexus.com/presentations/spring-modulith-what-the-what) project.

And, of course, at 4pm, I will be presenting a rip roaring look at [**Bootiful Spring Boot 3**](https://devnexus.com/presentations/bootiful-spring-boot-3). This is going to be a ton of fun and a great way to close out the day.

Yah. That's right. I said "the day." We're not done! There's still another amazing day - Thursday April 6th, 2023 - so let's take a look at that, now!

## [](#thursday-april-6th-2023)Thursday, April 6th, 2023

At 10am, Spring Cloud cofounder Spencer Gibb will have a presentation on [Spring Cloud Gateway: What is it and What's New](https://devnexus.com/presentations/spring-cloud-gateway-what-is-it-and-what-s-new). Tim Spann will also be presenting on [Apache Pulsar Development 101 with Java](https://devnexus.com/presentations/apache-pulsar-development-101-with-java), which I assume would include coverage of Spring for Apache Pulsar. Friend and legend DaShaun Carter will be doing a hilariously titled talk, [*My children will never deploy active-passive*](https://devnexus.com/presentations/my-children-will-never-deploy-active-passive).

At 11am, Chris Bono and Christophe Bornet will be introducing [Spring for Apache Pulsar](https://devnexus.com/presentations/introducing-spring-for-apache-pulsar). Cloud-native and my friend Whitney Lee will also be doing a talk introducing KNative, which is a powerful way to manage work [and workloads on Kubernetes](https://devnexus.com/presentations/knative-core-concepts-and-spiders).

At 1:30pm, Steve Riesenberg and Josh Cummings [will introduce Spring Security 6](https://devnexus.com/presentations/next-up-spring-security-6).

At 2:45pm, Spring Cloud Function lead Oleg Zhurakousky will [introduce Spring Cloud Stream: Past, Present, and Future](https://devnexus.com/presentations/spring-cloud-stream-past-present-and-future).

At 3:45pm, *Spring in Action* author Craig Walls will have a talk, [*Kontain Your Spring*](https://devnexus.com/presentations/kontain-your-spring). Observability legend Jonatan Ivanov will have a talk [*To production and Beyond: Observability for Modern Spring Applications*](https://devnexus.com/presentations/to-production-and-beyond-observability-for-modern-spring-applications). Théo Lebrun will be giving a talk [\_Creating a Modern Web App Using Spring Boot and Vue.js and JHipster](https://devnexus.com/presentations/creating-a-modern-web-app-using-spring-boot-and-vue-js-with-jhipster), which looks interesting!

Whew! What a roundup! There's a ton of fun to be had these few days! Make sure you stay hyrdated, take good notes, and I look forward to seeing you at 4pm on the 5th!
