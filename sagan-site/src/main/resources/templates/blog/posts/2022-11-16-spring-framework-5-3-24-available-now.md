# Spring Framework 5.3.24 available now

_Releases | Stéphane Nicoll |  November 16, 2022 | 2 Comments_

On behalf of the team and everyone who has contributed, I am pleased to announce that Spring Framework `5.3.24` is available now.

Spring Framework `5.3.24` includes [33 fixes and improvements](https://github.com/spring-projects/spring-framework/releases/tag/v5.3.24).
