# This Week in Sprng - April 1st, 2025

_Engineering | Josh Long |  April 01, 2025 | 0 Comments_

Hi, Spring fans! Welcome to another exciting installment of *This Week in Spring*! It's April Fools day, so be wary of things you read on the internet, but it's *also* the 11th anniversary of Spring Boot 1.0, which [was released this day in 2014! (that's not an April Fools)](https://spring.io/blog/2014/04/01/spring-boot-1-0-ga-released). Happy birthday!

I'm in excellent Austin, TX, at Dr. Venkat Subramaniam's phenomenal [Arc of AI](https://www.arcofai.com) show. I love this show! So many amazing people - many of whom also happen to be world-class speakers and engineers - converge in one place to synthesize their vantage point on "A.I." in one hour. Of course, I will be talking about Spring AI.

-   I just did a video [looking at how to use Spring AI with Amazon Bedrock for AWS](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=Y291afdLroQ) with the legendary James Ward, friend of *A Bootiful Podcast*, Java Champion, and friend of the community.
-   that video in [turn inspired a blog](https://community.aws/content/2v8AETAkyvPp9RVKC4YChncaEbs/running-mcp-based-agents-clients-servers-on-aws) titled "Running MCP Based Agents and Client-Servers on AWS"..
-   and that blog was in turn just linked to by [AWS Vice President Swami SivasubramanianSwami](https://www.linkedin.com/posts/swaminathansivasubramanian_agentic-systems-offer-possibilities-that-activity-7312929166201405440-EZv2?utm_source=share&utm_medium=member_desktop&rcm=ACoAAACXdIABb8VbfXttpb7WA9oliQsXOS5rEgw) announcing new AWS-related MCP integrations. Awesome! Thanks for the opportunity to collaborate, AWS! Peanut and Prancer, forever!
-   in last week's installment of *A Bootiful Podcast*, I talked to my friend [Anthony Dahanne about build packs, production, Docker, and so much more](https://spring.io/blog/2025/03/27/a-bootiful-podcast-anthony-dahanne)
-   there are some essential and breaking changes [in Spring AI `main`](https://spring.io/blog/2025/03/25/spring-ai-update-to-snapshots)
-   I love this! My longtime friend, industry analyst, pundit, and thought lord extraordinaire Michael Coté looks at using Spring AI to build an MCP service with Spring AI's MCP support! This is an [Amazing video](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=iROihhd_OiI) from a guy who does fantastic things.
-   JavaCodeGeeks has a nice [post on One Time Token login](https://www.javacodegeeks.com/spring-security-one-time-token-login-example.html)
-   I may be a bit biased, and this video doesn't leave a lot of room for the nuance required to have these sorts of discussions, but [I found this video hilarious](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=nKE8pqvhrs8). That said, I disagree with the premise because it's absurd on the face :-) Spring Boot is a sound synthesis of several different concerns. I, just Josh Long, might even say it's the best. But that's not to say that other technologies don't do certain things better or that other technologies didn't do certain things first...

-   [the Java 24 launch keynote from JavaOne is now available](https://www.youtube.com/live/mk_2MIWxLI0?t=3488s)
