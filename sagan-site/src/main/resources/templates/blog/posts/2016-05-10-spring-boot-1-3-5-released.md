# Spring Boot 1.3.5 released

_Releases | Stéphane Nicoll |  May 10, 2016 | 0 Comments_

Shortly after 1.3.4, we've just released [Spring Boot 1.3.5](https://github.com/spring-projects/spring-boot/issues?q=milestone%3A1.3.5+is%3Aclosed) as we've identified a [rather nasty regression](https://github.com/spring-projects/spring-boot/issues/5901) with the Spring Integration auto-configuration. The bits are available from [repo.spring.io](http://repo.spring.io/release) and [Maven Central](http://search.maven.org/#search%7Cga%7C1%7Cg%3A%22org.springframework.boot%22), as usual.
