# This Week in Spring - October 14th, 2025

_Engineering | Josh Long |  October 14, 2025 | 0 Comments_

Hi, Spring fans! Welcome to another installment of *This Week in Spring*! I'm in Boston and New York city and Bulgaria and Poland this week, but I'm still hyped about last week's *amazing* Devoxx Belgium event! There are a *ton* of videos to look at, and I'll include just some of them here.

So, without further ado, let's dive into it!

-   Nice! [Micrometer releases: 1.14.12, 1.15.5, 1.16.0-RC1](https://github.com/micrometer-metrics/micrometer/releases)
-   And ... [Micrometer Tracing releases: 1.4.11, 1.5.5, 1.6.0-RC1](https://github.com/micrometer-metrics/tracing/releases)
-   the road to GA is long and amazing, and this newest post by Soby Chacko looks at the [new shared consumer support in Spring for Apache Kafka](https://spring.io/blog/2025/10/14/introducing-spring-kafka-share-consumer)
-   good news everybody! [Spring Session Hazelcast is now being led by the Hazelcast team](https://spring.io/blog/2025/10/14/spring-session-hazelcast-new-leadership)
-   and in the same vein, [Spring Session MongoDB is now led by the MongoDB team!](https://spring.io/blog/2025/10/14/spring-session-mongodb-new-leadership)
-   [Spring Batch 6.0.0 M4 has been released](https://spring.io/blog/2025/10/09/spring-batch-6-0-0-m4-released)
-   in last week's installment of *A Bootiful Podcast*, I talked to [Spring Security legend Josh Cummings](https://spring.io/blog/2025/10/09/a-bootiful-podcast-josh-cummings)
-   I loved this post from Sebastien Deleuze that looks at the brand [new Jackson 3 support in Spring Framework 7](https://spring.io/blog/2025/10/07/introducing-jackson-3-support-in-spring)
-   want to contribute to the white hot Spring AI project? [Start here](https://spring.io/blog/2025/10/07/spring-ai-community-announcement)
-   I love this blog from CowTownCoder (the lead engineer on the Jackson JSON marshalling library) [about Jackson 3.0](https://cowtowncoder.medium.com/jackson-3-0-0-ga-released-1f669cda529a) with a nice shout-out to the Spring team
-   interesting article on running [durable agents with Spring AI on Dapr workflows](https://dzone.com/articles/durable-agents-with-spring-ai-and-dapr)
-   this is, as usual, an amazing talk from Spring Security contributor and legend [Daniel Garnier Moiroux](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=LGlyLmxjutI)
-   and I love this one from Buildpacks contributor [Anthony Dahanne](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=RX9zwgHuNmA)
-   and I love this post on optimizing applications in the cloud with [Spring Boot by community legend Patrick Baumgartner](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=cbs4BQ0BwmA)
-   tactical domain driven design with [Java and Spring, by Oliver Drotbohm](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=k3o1nSh3g2Q)
-   and I loved this talk by Spring founder Rod Johnson on building agents on [the JVM with Rod's new framework, called Embabel](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=dbx1_P7W1DM). Embabel, of course, is powered by Spring AI, and I love it!
-   Spring team alumnus Arjen Poutsma did an amazing talk called [*A Long Spring: 19 years of living with your past mistakes*](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=0OtngsVzOno).
-   and of course, I did a talk [looking at Spring Boot 4 and Spring Framework 7](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=KwXMAtldEz0)
