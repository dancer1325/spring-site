# Spring HATEOAS 1.2.6 and 1.3.1 released

_Engineering | Oliver Drotbohm |  May 12, 2021 | 0 Comments_

On behalf of the Spring community I’d like to announce the availability of Spring HATEOAS 1.2.6 and 1.3.1. The releases contain general bugfixes and are scheduled for inclusion in the upcoming Spring Boot service releases.

Find the detailed changelogs for 1.2.6 [here](https://github.com/spring-projects/spring-hateoas/releases/tag/1.2.6) and the one for 1.3.1 [here](https://github.com/spring-projects/spring-hateoas/releases/tag/1.3.1).
