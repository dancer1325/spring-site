# Spring Tips: Season 1

_Engineering | Josh Long |  December 28, 2016 | 0 Comments_

Hi Spring fans! I'm taking some time off for the holiday so there won't be a new Spring Tips until after the new year. If you're itching for something to watch and learn, here are some resources to tide you over.

*Happy new year!*

-   [the Spring YouTube channel](http://spring.io/video) is a *wonderful* resource with countless talks and videos from previous years' SpringOne2GX, SpringOne Platform and Cloud Foundry summit conferences, our year-long webinar program and even various meetups and so on. This is one of my absolute favorite resources that, thanks to [our own Pieter Humphrey](https://twitter.com/PieterHumphrey), has grown in *leaps and bounds* in the last several years, exceeding even the Java YouTube channel in subscribers!
-   [the Spring Tips YouTube Playlist](https://www.youtube.com/playlist?list=PLgGXSWYM2FpPw8rV0tZoMiJYSCiLhPnOc) - all the previous installments of Spring Tips are captured in this playlist. If you missed an episode or simply want to review, start here! So far, in 2016, we've looked at [Spring Integration](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=MTKlk8_9aAw&index=1&list=PLgGXSWYM2FpPw8rV0tZoMiJYSCiLhPnOc&t=1934s), [Kotlin](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=90WRtrbRi0Y&index=2&list=PLgGXSWYM2FpPw8rV0tZoMiJYSCiLhPnOc&t=19s), [Circuit Breakers](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=Kc7dDxn9cUg&index=3&list=PLgGXSWYM2FpPw8rV0tZoMiJYSCiLhPnOc&t=43s), [Spring-aware beans](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=O8nIzE99BJY&index=4&list=PLgGXSWYM2FpPw8rV0tZoMiJYSCiLhPnOc&t=448s), [functional reactive endpoints in Spring 5](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=JjrAmhlTjug&index=5&list=PLgGXSWYM2FpPw8rV0tZoMiJYSCiLhPnOc&t=928s), [Spring Cloud Stream](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=HQ00E60kB6c&index=6&list=PLgGXSWYM2FpPw8rV0tZoMiJYSCiLhPnOc&t=983s), [Cloud Foundry](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=T9KcS8s0RPs&index=7&list=PLgGXSWYM2FpPw8rV0tZoMiJYSCiLhPnOc&t=106s), [JTA](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=356eqR8XcqE&index=8&list=PLgGXSWYM2FpPw8rV0tZoMiJYSCiLhPnOc&t=23s), [Bootiful Dashboards](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=u1QnlAbCFys&index=9&list=PLgGXSWYM2FpPw8rV0tZoMiJYSCiLhPnOc&t=942s), [Demystifying Spring Boot](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=Sw7I70vjN0E&index=10&list=PLgGXSWYM2FpPw8rV0tZoMiJYSCiLhPnOc&t=22s), and [Spring Cloud Data Flow](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=GaHgRw-3K84&index=11&list=PLgGXSWYM2FpPw8rV0tZoMiJYSCiLhPnOc&t=12s)
-   [the Spring Guides](http://spring.io/guides) - here you'll find all sorts of concise, topical guides introducing a variety of different topics related to Spring and Cloud Foundry. Scroll to the bottom for longer-form tutorials and higher level overivews of different aspects of the Spring ecosystem.
