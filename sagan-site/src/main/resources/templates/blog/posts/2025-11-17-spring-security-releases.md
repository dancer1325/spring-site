# Spring Security 2025-11 Releases - 7.0.0, 6.5.7, 6.4.13 available now!

_Releases | Rob Winch |  November 17, 2025 | 0 Comments_

The highlights of 7.0.0 can be found in the [What's New](https://docs.spring.io/spring-security/reference/7.0/whats-new.html) Section of the reference.
