# Spring Framework 6.1.12, 6.0.23, and 5.3.39 Available Now

_Releases | Stéphane Nicoll |  August 14, 2024 | 1 Comment_

On behalf of the team and everyone who has contributed, I am pleased to announce that Spring Framework `6.1.12`, `6.0.23` and `5.3.39` are available now. While `5.3.38` has also been released, it got superseded by `5.3.39`.

-   Spring Framework `6.1.12` ships with [43 fixes and documentation improvements](https://github.com/spring-projects/spring-framework/releases/tag/v6.1.12). This version will be shipped with [Spring Boot 3.2.9](https://github.com/spring-projects/spring-boot/milestone/354) and [Spring Boot 3.3.3](https://github.com/spring-projects/spring-boot/milestone/355), to be released next week.
-   Spring Framework `6.0.23` ships with [14 fixes and documentation improvements](https://github.com/spring-projects/spring-framework/releases/tag/v6.0.23).
-   Spring Framework `5.3.39` ships with [1 fixes](https://github.com/spring-projects/spring-framework/releases/tag/v5.3.39) plus the [12 fixes and documentation improvements of 5.3.38](https://github.com/spring-projects/spring-framework/releases/tag/v5.3.38).

Please note that OSS support for `5.3.x` and `6.0.x` ends on August 31, 2024. As such, `5.3.39` and `6.0.23` are meant to be the last OSS releases. Commercial support [is available](https://enterprise.spring.io/).
