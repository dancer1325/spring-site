# Spring Framework 6.0.0-M4 available now

_Releases | Brian Clozel |  May 12, 2022 | 6 Comments_

On behalf of the team and everyone who has contributed, I am pleased to announce that Spring Framework `6.0.0-M4` is available now.

Spring Framework `6.0.0-M4` ships with [all the fixes from 5.3.20 released yesterday](https://spring.io/blog/2022/05/11/spring-framework-5-3-20-and-5-2-22-available-now), and also includes [39 fixes and improvements](https://github.com/spring-projects/spring-framework/releases/tag/v6.0.0-M4) specific to the 6.0 branch.
