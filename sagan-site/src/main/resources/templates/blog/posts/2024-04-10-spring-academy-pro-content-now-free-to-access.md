# Spring Academy Pro Content Now Free To Access

_News | Michael Minella |  April 10, 2024 | 11 Comments_

We’re excited to share some great news! Spring Academy Pro will no longer require a paid subscription. It will be available [here](https://spring.academy/) to everyone who registers a work, vocational, or educational email address.

## [](#what-is-spring-academy)What is Spring Academy?

Spring Academy is a comprehensive online learning platform for Spring developers. Created by the stewards of the Spring portfolio, Spring Academy courses ensure that you learn the most accurate and up-to-date information, with a hands-on learning experience that allows you to apply what you've learned and gain the skills necessary to create robust, scalable, and maintainable applications.

The browser-based lab environment gives you access to the tools you need to complete lab exercises with ease and includes all courses and lessons on the Spring Academy site, now with complimentary inclusion of Pro content, video lessons, and hands-on labs.

## [](#how-to-get-started)How to Get Started

[Sign up today](https://spring.academy/) and instantly leverage the power of Spring with guidance from the founders and experts of the framework.
