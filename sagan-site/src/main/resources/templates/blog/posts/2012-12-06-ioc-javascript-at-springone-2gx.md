# IOC + Javascript at SpringOne 2GX

_Engineering | John Hann |  December 06, 2012 | 0 Comments_

by [Brian Cavalier](http://blog.springsource.org/author/bcavalier/) & [John Hann](http://blog.springsource.org/author/jhann/)

We gave a talk on IOC + Javascript at [SpringOne 2GX](http://springone2gx.com/conference/washington/2012/10/home). Front-end web application architecture seemed to be quite a hot topic at the conference, in general, and our talk seemed to be very well received. Here are the [video](http://www.youtube.com/watch?v=TqX-CqYYwEc) and [slides](http://www.slideshare.net/briancavalier/ioc-javascript) from the talk.

We'll also be starting a blog series about architectural patterns for modern Javascript web apps, featuring posts from members of SpringSource's new front-end architecture team, S2JS. The posts will expand on the concepts presented in our talk, as well as those presented by [Scott Andrews](http://blog.springsource.org/author/andrewss/) and [Jeremy Grelle](http://blog.springsource.org/author/jgrelle/) in their talks.

The first post in the series is coming next week. Until then, we hope you enjoy the [video](http://www.youtube.com/watch?v=TqX-CqYYwEc) and the [slides](http://www.slideshare.net/briancavalier/ioc-javascript).  If you'd like to get a head start, you can browse the demo app's [source code](https://github.com/five-eleven/monty-hall-ui).
