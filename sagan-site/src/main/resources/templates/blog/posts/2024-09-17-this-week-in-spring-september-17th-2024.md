# This Week in Spring - September 17th, 2024

_Engineering | Josh Long |  September 17, 2024 | 0 Comments_

Hi, Spring fans! Last week I was in scintilliating Seoul, Korea, and then tantalizing Tokyo, Japan, and now I'm in marvelous Mumbai, India, at the airport, actually, headed to New Delhi, India. It's been a busy week for me and even busier a week for the community, so let's dive into it!

-   [Java 23 is here! Happy Java 23 day to those who celebrate!](https://www.oracle.com/news/announcement/oracle-releases-java-23-2024-09-17/) Now to wait for [SDKMAN](https://sdkman.io) to pick it up...
-   In last week's podcast episode, I (*finally!*) got to sit [down with Spring creator Rod Johnson](https://spring.io/blog/2024/09/12/spring-creator-and-cofounder-dr-rod-johnson).
-   [Spring Tools has some cool new upgrades](https://x.com/springtools4/status/1835680743525122488?s=12&t=n-UflcIbnx1lage-TBk0Cg).
-   [I love this documentary looking at the success of Kotlin](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=E8CtE7qTb-Q).
-   Nice tip from Spring community member and JetBrains developer advocate Pasha Finkelshteyn: when you put a cron pattern like `* 2 */3 * * 2` inside an `@Scheduled` annotation in a Spring application, [IntelliJ IDEA explains what you just wrote!](https://x.com/asm0di0/status/1834478157870628960?s=12). Speaking of IntelliJ, the new EAP release [is out and has some truly cool AI-centric features](https://x.com/intellijidea/status/1834170229951176985?s=12).
-   Spring, Kotlin, and WebAssembly legend Sébastien Deleuze has updated his sample [app demonstrating a litany of amazing new features](https://x.com/sdeleuze/status/1832806306027077985?s=12&t=n-UflcIbnx1lage-TBk0Cg).
-   Just a friendly reminder, I've got a podcast that JetBrains ranked as one [of the best podcasts for programmers](https://blog.jetbrains.com/idea/2024/09/top-12-podcasts-for-java-developers-in-2024/) — like and subscribe?

-   [Spring Framework 5.3.40 and 6.0.24 available now](https://spring.io/blog/2024/09/12/spring-framework-5-3-40-and-6-0-24-available-now).
