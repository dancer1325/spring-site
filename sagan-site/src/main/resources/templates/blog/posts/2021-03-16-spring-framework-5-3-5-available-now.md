# Spring Framework 5.3.5 available now

_Releases | Stéphane Nicoll |  March 16, 2021 | 3 Comments_

On behalf of the team and everyone who has contributed, I am pleased to announce that Spring Framework `5.3.5` is available now.

Spring Framework `5.3.5` includes [39 fixes and improvements](https://github.com/spring-projects/spring-framework/releases/tag/v5.3.5) and covers support for JDK 16 (which is scheduled for GA today), in preparation for JDK 17 LTS in September.

Stay tuned for follow-up Spring Boot releases later this week.
