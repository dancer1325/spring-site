# Spring Framework 5.3.21 available now

_Releases | Stéphane Nicoll |  June 15, 2022 | 0 Comments_

On behalf of the team and everyone who has contributed, I am pleased to announce that Spring Framework `5.3.21` is available now.

Spring Framework `5.3.21` includes [22 fixes and improvements](https://github.com/spring-projects/spring-framework/releases/tag/v5.3.21).
