# SpringOne 2GX 2010 Tech Talk: Groovy.DSLs (from: beginner, to: expert)

_News | Adam Fitzgerald |  February 18, 2011 | 0 Comments_

[![SpringOne 2GX 2010 Tech Talk: Groovy DSLs](/files/SpringOne2GX2010GuillaumeLaforgePaulKing.png)](http://www.infoq.com/presentations/Groovy-DSLs)

[InfoQ](http://www.infoq.com/SpringOne_2010) has released another talk from [SpringOne 2GX 2010](http://www.springone2gx.com), this one covers [Groovy.DSLs (from: beginner, to: expert)](http://www.infoq.com/presentations/Groovy-DSLs). In this presentation, [Groovy](http://groovy.codehaus.org) experts [Guillaume Laforge](http://blog.springsource.com/author/glaforge/) and Paul King give a detailed introduction and practical guide to using Groovy to create your domain specific languages.

Guillaume and Paul Mark provide:

-   A myriad of DSL examples from specialized computing requirements to general business tasks
-   Characteristics to keep in mind when creating your own DSL
-   Details about Groovy's DSL advantages including flexible and malleable syntax, runtime meta-programming, regular expression pre-processing as well as being a Java based runtime
-   Pitfalls and problems to avoid with concrete examples

Many thanks to InfoQ for [covering SpringOne 2GX 2010](http://www.infoq.com/SpringOne_2010) and providing this outstanding presentations to the community.
