# Spring Framework 6.0.1 available now

_Releases | Stéphane Nicoll |  November 23, 2022 | 0 Comments_

On behalf of the team and everyone who has contributed, I am pleased to announce that Spring Framework `6.0.1` is available now.

Spring Framework `6.0.1` includes [21 fixes and improvements](https://github.com/spring-projects/spring-framework/releases/tag/v6.0.1).
