# This Week in Spring - August 9th, 2016

_Engineering | Josh Long |  August 09, 2016 | 2 Comments_

Welcome to another installment of *This Week in Spring*! This week I'm recovering from a *crazy* awesome week at SpringOne Platform while visiting customers here in summer-time London.

We've got a lot to cover so let's get to it!

-   the [IntelliJ IDEA folks have updated their coverage on Spring](https://blog.jetbrains.com/idea/2016/08/webinar-recording-whats-new-in-intellij-idea-2016-2-for-spring-developers)
-   there were several wrapup blogs from last week's SpringOne Platform event, including the two we published on this very blog for [day 1](https://spring.io/blog/2016/08/03/springone-platform-2016-recap-day-1)

and [day 2](https://spring.io/blog/2016/08/03/springone-platform-2016-recap-day-2) and the posts on Pivotal's main blog for [day 1](https://blog.pivotal.io/pivotal-cloud-foundry/features/springone-platform-a-movement-comes-of-age), [day 2](https://blog.pivotal.io/pivotal-cloud-foundry/features/taking-on-the-monolith-with-people-and-tech-springone-platform-day-2), [day 3](https://blog.pivotal.io/big-data-pivotal/features/positive-sum-software-springs-new-bounce-springone-platform-day-3), and [final call](https://blog.pivotal.io/pivotal-cloud-foundry/features/final-call-parting-shots-from-springone-platform)

-   while we're at the Pivotal blog, those of you [wondering about Blockchain on Cloud Foundry might appreciate this post](https://blog.pivotal.io/pivotal-cloud-foundry/products/blockchain-use-cases-for-blockchain-on-cloud-foundry)
-   SpringOne Platform was filled with *excellent* keynotes, all of which will be available online already (or soon). Naturally, you *have* to watch [Adrian Cockroft (@adrianco)](http://twitter.com/adrianco) 's excellent [*Simplifying the Future* keynote](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=DGK6jjamzfY&feature=youtu.be): "Let's just do Spring Boot on Cloud Foundry."

Check out this InfoQ interview with Pivotal's [own Mark Thomas](https://www.infoq.com/news/2016/08/tomcat9-vs-javaee8?utm_source=infoq&utm_medium=popular_widget&utm_campaign=popular_content_list&utm_content=homepage)
