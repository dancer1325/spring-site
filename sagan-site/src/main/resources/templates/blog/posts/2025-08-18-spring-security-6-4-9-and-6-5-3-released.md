# Spring Security 6.4.9 and 6.5.3 Released

_Releases | Josh Cummings |  August 18, 2025 | 0 Comments_

On behalf of the team and everyone who has contributed, I am pleased to announce the availability of Spring Security `6.4.9` and `6.5.3`.

Spring Security `6.4.9` ships with [5 fixes and several dependency upgrades](https://github.com/spring-projects/spring-security/releases/tag/6.4.9). This version will be shipped this week with Spring Boot `3.4.9`.

Spring Security `6.5.3` ships with [8 fixes and several dependency upgrades](https://github.com/spring-projects/spring-security/releases/tag/6.5.3). This version will be shipped this week with Spring Boot `3.5.5`.
