# Spring Boot 1.4.0.M3 Available Now

_Releases | Phil Webb |  May 17, 2016 | 2 Comments_

Spring Boot v1.4.0.M3 is available now from the [Spring milestone repository](http://repo.spring.io/milestone/). This release closes over [150 issues](https://github.com/spring-projects/spring-boot/issues?q=milestone%3A1.4.0.M3+is%3Aclosed) and introduces a number of new features.

Highlights include:

-   Lots of bug fixes and improvements over M2 (thanks to everyone for trying the milestones).
-   Convention based error pages (want a custom 404, just add `src/main/resources/public/error/404.html`).
-   Improved ErrorPage registration support.
-   Support for pluggable OAuth2 Principal extraction.

For a complete list of changes, and upgrade instructions, see the [Spring Boot 1.4 Release Notes](https://github.com/spring-projects/spring-boot/wiki/Spring-Boot-1.4-Release-Notes) on the WIKI and the updated [reference documentation](http://docs.spring.io/spring-boot/docs/1.4.0.M3/reference/htmlsingle/).

As always, thanks to all our contributors and please continue to provide feedback and [bug reports](https://github.com/spring-projects/spring-boot/issues).

Hopefully we'll see a few of you at the [Spring I/O conference](http://springio.net/) in Barcelona this week, or at [SpringOne Platform in August](https://springoneplatform.io/)!
