# Spring Framework 5.2.7 and 5.1.16 available now

_Releases | Stéphane Nicoll |  June 09, 2020 | 0 Comments_

On behalf of the team and everyone who has contributed, I am pleased to announce that Spring Framework `5.2.7` and `5.1.16` are available now.

Spring Framework `5.2.7` includes [over 44 fixes and improvements](https://github.com/spring-projects/spring-framework/releases/tag/v5.2.7.RELEASE). Spring Framework `5.1.16` [includes 17 selected fixes and improvements](https://github.com/spring-projects/spring-framework/releases/tag/v5.1.16.RELEASE).

As usual, we'll follow up shortly with corresponding Spring Boot releases (2.3.1, 2.2.8 and 2.1.15).
