# Spring Modulith 1.1.6 and 1.2.1 released

_Releases | Oliver Drotbohm |  June 21, 2024 | 2 Comments_

I am delighted to announce the availability of Spring Modulith 1.1.6 and 1.2.1. The releases contain a few bug fixes, minor new features and the usual dependency upgrades. The highlights are the following:

-   The ability to configure an `ApplicationModuleDetectionStrategy` via `spring.modulith.detection-strategy` – [GH-656](https://github.com/spring-projects/spring-modulith/issues/656).
-   Fixes in dependency verifications involving named interfaces – [GH-661](https://github.com/spring-projects/spring-modulith/issues/661), [GH-662](https://github.com/spring-projects/spring-modulith/issues/662).
-   Upgrades to Spring Boot 3.2.7 ([GH-675](https://github.com/spring-projects/spring-modulith/issues/675)) and 3.3.1 ([GH-678](https://github.com/spring-projects/spring-modulith/issues/678)).

Find more information about the releases in the full change logs for [1.1.6](https://github.com/spring-projects/spring-modulith/releases/tag/1.1.6) and [1.2.1](https://github.com/spring-projects/spring-modulith/releases/tag/1.2.1).
