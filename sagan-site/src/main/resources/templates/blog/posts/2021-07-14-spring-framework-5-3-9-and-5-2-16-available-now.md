# Spring Framework 5.3.9 and 5.2.16 available now

_Releases | Stéphane Nicoll |  July 14, 2021 | 0 Comments_

On behalf of the team and everyone who has contributed, I am pleased to announce that Spring Framework `5.3.9` and `5.2.16` are available now.

Spring Framework `5.3.9` includes [45 fixes and improvements](https://github.com/spring-projects/spring-framework/releases/tag/v5.3.9). Spring Framework `5.2.16` includes [12 selected fixes and improvements](https://github.com/spring-projects/spring-framework/releases/tag/v5.2.16.RELEASE).

Stay tuned for follow-up Spring Boot releases next week.
