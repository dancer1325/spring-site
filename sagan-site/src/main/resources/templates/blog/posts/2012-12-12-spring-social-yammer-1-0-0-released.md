# Spring Social Yammer 1.0.0 Released

_Releases | Craig Walls |  December 12, 2012 | 0 Comments_

Dear Spring Community,

I'm happy to share the news that Morten Andersen-Gott (aka, @mortenag on Twitter or magott on GitHub) has released Spring Social Yammer 1.0.0. Spring Social Yammer is one of over 25 community-led extension to Spring Social. It brings Spring Social-style connection and API binding support to applications needing to connect with Yammer.

Spring Social Yammer can be found at the following links:

-   [Release Distribution](http://www.springsource.org/download/community?project=Spring%2520Social%2520Yammer)
-   [Reference Documentation](http://static.springsource.org/spring-social-yammer/docs/1.0.0.RELEASE/reference/htmlsingle/)
-   [API Documentation (JavaDoc)](http://static.springsource.org/spring-social-yammer/docs/1.0.0.RELEASE/api/)
-   [Source Code](https://github.com/magott/spring-social-yammer)

Spring Social Yammer is also available in the Maven Central repository for inclusion as a [dependency in your Maven or Gradle builds](http://static.springsource.org/spring-social-yammer/docs/1.0.0.RELEASE/reference/htmlsingle/#overview-howtoget).

Morten announced the release via Twitter at [http://twitter.com/mortenag/status/278794566755299328](http://twitter.com/mortenag/status/278794566755299328). Please join me in congratulating him on his work by replying to or retweeting his announcement.
