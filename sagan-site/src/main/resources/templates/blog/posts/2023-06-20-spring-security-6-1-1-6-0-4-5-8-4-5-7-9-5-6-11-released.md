# Spring Security 6.1.1, 6.0.4, 5.8.4, 5.7.9, 5.6.11 Released

_Releases | Rob Winch |  June 20, 2023 | 0 Comments_

On behalf of the team and everyone who has contributed, I am pleased to announce that Spring Security 6.1.1, 6.0.4, 5.8.4, 5.7.9, and 5.6.11 are available now. The releases are mostly composed of bug fixes, dependency upgrades, and documentation improvements.

To learn more, please visit the [6.1.1](https://github.com/spring-projects/spring-security/releases/tag/6.1.1), [6.0.4](https://github.com/spring-projects/spring-security/releases/tag/6.0.4), [5.8.4](https://github.com/spring-projects/spring-security/releases/tag/5.8.4), [5.7.9](https://github.com/spring-projects/spring-security/releases/tag/5.7.9), and [5.6.11](https://github.com/spring-projects/spring-security/releases/tag/5.6.11) release summaries.
