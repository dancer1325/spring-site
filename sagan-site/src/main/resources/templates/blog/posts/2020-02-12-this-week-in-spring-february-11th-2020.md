# This Week in Spring - February 11th, 2020

_Engineering | Josh Long |  February 12, 2020 | 2 Comments_

Hi, Spring fans! Welcome to yet another installment of *This Week in Spring*! It's been a very nice week indeed. I've been knee-deep in finishing my book, writing more code to [support the *A Bootiful Podcast*](http://BootifulPodcast.fm) production pipeline, and meeting amazing people in the new VMWare organization, and beyond. I've also been working on some new [Spring Tips](http://bit.ly/spring-tips-playlist) videos - [what would you like to see covered](http://twitter.com/starbuxman), my friends?

We've got a lot of good stuff this week, so let's get to it!
