# Spring Security 6.5.8, 7.0.3 and 7.1.0-M2 available now

_Releases | Joe Grandja |  February 13, 2026 | 0 Comments_

On behalf of the team and everyone who has contributed, I am pleased to announce the releases of Spring Security `6.5.8`, `7.0.3` and `7.1.0-M2`.

Please see the Spring Security [6.5.8](https://github.com/spring-projects/spring-security/releases/tag/6.5.8), [7.0.3](https://github.com/spring-projects/spring-security/releases/tag/7.0.3) and [7.1.0-M2](https://github.com/spring-projects/spring-security/releases/tag/7.1.0-M2) release notes for more details.
