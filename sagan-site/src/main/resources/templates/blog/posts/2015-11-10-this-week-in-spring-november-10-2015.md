# This Week in Spring - November, 10, 2015

_Engineering | Josh Long |  November 10, 2015 | 0 Comments_

Welcome to another installation of *This Week in Spring*! This week I'm in Antwerp, Belgium for the grand mother of all Devoxxes (Devoxxen?), Devoxx Belgium! I'm here along with several members of the team and [if you're around, don't hesitate to say hi](http://twitter.com/starbuxman)!

-   [Dr. Mark Pollack has just announced the release of Spring XD 1.3RC1](https://spring.io/blog/2015/11/03/spring-xd-1-3-rc1-released)
-   Stormpath, the cloud, and on-premise, identity provider as-a-service has put up [an *exhaustive* blog on using their Spring Boot auto-configuration with Spring Security](https://stormpath.com/blog/build-spring-boot-spring-security-app/)! This is a *really* cool integration. I hope people reward their hard work in striving for the most powerful, expressive, and convenient developer experience! Way to go!
-   Check out this short-and-sweet article on DZone [to learn about Spring Boot's auto-configuration mechanism](https://dzone.com/articles/under-the-boot)
-   The Spring framework Guru is back with a video tutorial [on using Thymeleaf to create an index page](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=NNYB7G-WP5s)
-   Check out our own mad-scientist Andy Clement's video [introducing Flo, a UI for batch job orchestration on Spring XD!](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=kr_79McGjHY)
-   The *CoffeaElectronica* blog has a [post on using and extending the Spring Boot Remote Shell](http://coffeaelectronica.com/blog/2015/spring-shell.html)
-   Check out Adib Saikali's epic [InfoQ article on using Spring Session](http://www.infoq.com/articles/Next-Generation-Session-Management-with-Spring-Session) [http://www.infoq.com/articles/Next-Generation-Session-Management-with-Spring-Session](http://www.infoq.com/articles/Next-Generation-Session-Management-with-Spring-Session)
-   our pal Ryan Baxter has posted another installment [in his series on building cloud-native apps with Spring Boot](http://ryanjbaxter.com/2015/11/04/building-cloud-native-apps-with-spring-part-7/)
-   This isn't strictly Spring related, but interesting: Matt Raible's post on InfoQ looks at [Google's *much faster* mirror of Maven Central](http://www.infoq.com/news/2015/11/maven-central-at-google)
-   Also, Atlassian has a nice [ad-article on continuous-integration as the basis of continuous delivery](https://www.atlassian.com/continuous-delivery/nuts-and-bolts-continuous-integration), which is a *very* powerful thing to grok. This was a nice short primer so I figured I'd pass it on
