_Releases | Adam Fitzgerald |  September 25, 2009 | 0 Comments_

This news just in from [Arjen Poutsma](http://blog.springsource.com/author/arjenp/)'s [twitter feed](http://twitter.com/poutsma): Spring 3.0.0.RC1 is now available.
