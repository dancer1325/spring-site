# Spring Shell 2.1.5 and 3.0.0-RC1 are now available

_Releases | Janne Valkealahti |  January 13, 2023 | 0 Comments_

On behalf of the team and everyone who has contributed, I'm happy to announce that Spring Shell `2.1.5` and `3.0.0-RC1` has been released and is now available from [https://repo.spring.io/release](https://repo.spring.io/release), Maven Central and [https://repo.spring.io/milestone](https://repo.spring.io/milestone) respectively.

Please see the [release notes 2.1.5](https://github.com/spring-projects/spring-shell/releases/tag/v2.1.5) and [release notes 3.0.0-RC1](https://github.com/spring-projects/spring-shell/releases/tag/v3.0.0-RC1) for more details.

Thanks to all those who have contributed with issue reports and pull requests.
