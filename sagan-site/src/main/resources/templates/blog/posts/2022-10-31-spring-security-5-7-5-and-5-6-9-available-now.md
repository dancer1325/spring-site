# Spring Security 5.7.5 and 5.6.9 available now

_Releases | Steve Riesenberg |  October 31, 2022 | 3 Comments_

On behalf of the team and everyone who has contributed, I am pleased to announce that Spring Security 5.7.5 and 5.6.9 are available now. In both cases the releases are composed of bug fixes.

To learn more, please visit the [5.7.5](https://github.com/spring-projects/spring-security/releases/tag/5.7.5) and [5.6.9](https://github.com/spring-projects/spring-security/releases/tag/5.6.9) release summaries.
