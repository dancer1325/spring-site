# SpringSource manifest headers registered with OSGi

_Engineering | Glyn Normington |  November 27, 2008 | 0 Comments_

The OSGi Alliance has decided to accommodate vendor-specific manifest headers by listing them in a [public registry](http://www.osgi.org/Specifications/ReferenceHeaders). The aim is to avoid clashes between vendors and between vendors and OSGi's own headers.

The registry currently contains OSGi's own headers, those introduced by [SpringSource dm Server](http://www.springsource.com/products/suite/dmserver), and two used by the [bnd](http://www.aqute.biz/Code/Bnd) tool.
