# This Week in Spring - November 29th, 2022 (Spring Boot 3 has arrived)

_Engineering | Josh Long |  November 29, 2022 | 0 Comments_

Hi, Spring fans! It's here! It's finally here, at long last! [Spring Boot 3!](https://spring.io/blog/2022/11/24/spring-boot-3-0-goes-ga)! And of course with Spring Boot 3.0 comes a whole portfolio of integrated projects that have also been updated! Remember, a *huge* theme in this release is support for GraalVM native images, and that's now supported across the board. You know what to do: [start.spring.io](https://start.spring.io).

I put together a Spring Tipds video introducing some of the [major themes of Spring Framework 6 / Spring Boot 3](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=aUm5WZjh8RA&t=1s0).

I also did an in-depth look at [the new GraalVM support powered by Spring's ahead-of-time (AOT) engine](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=TOfYlLjXufw&t=4s).

There's so much else to talk about this week, but I really can't stress this enough: Spring Boot 3 is *here*! In some ways, it feels like this release has been more than four years in the making! It's amazing, and I can't wait for people to get to production with it. Without further ado, this week's roundup:
