# Spring Security 6.4.8 and 6.5.2 Released

_Releases | Josh Cummings |  July 21, 2025 | 0 Comments_

On behalf of the team and everyone who has contributed, I am pleased to announce the availability of Spring Security `6.4.8` and `6.5.2`.

Spring Security `6.4.8` ships with [3 fixes and several dependency upgrades](https://github.com/spring-projects/spring-security/releases/tag/6.4.8). This version will be shipped next week with Spring Boot `3.4.8`.

Spring Security `6.5.2` ships with [7 fixes and several dependency upgrades](https://github.com/spring-projects/spring-security/releases/tag/6.5.2). This version will be shipped next week with Spring Boot `3.5.4`.
