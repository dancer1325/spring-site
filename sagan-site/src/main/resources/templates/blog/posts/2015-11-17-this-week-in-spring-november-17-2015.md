# This Week in Spring - November 17, 2015

_Engineering | Josh Long |  November 17, 2015 | 0 Comments_

My goodness the time has flown! We're already staring down the beginning of 2016!

Welcome to another installation of *This Week in Spring*! This week I'm in beautiful Casablanca, Morocco for Devoxx Morocco! This is the fifth Devoxx this year - with events in Belgium, UK, France, Poland and now Morocco - and I have the rare honor of having spoken at all of them! #fullDevoxx

We've got a lot to cover this week so let's get to it!

-   [**Spring Boot 1.3 has just been released**](http://spring.io/blog/2015/11/16/spring-boot-1-3-0-released)! This is the most epic release of Spring Boot yet and I won't even *try* to do the release notes! Just go check it out and visit the [Spring Initialzr](http://start.spring.io)
-   [Spring framework 4.2.3 has just been released](http://spring.io/blog/2015/11/16/spring-framework-4-2-3-available-now)!
-   Netflix have just announced the open-sourcing [of their build-and-bake pipeline for continuous delivery - project Spinnaker](http://techblog.netflix.com/2015/11/global-continuous-delivery-with.html). It's interesting for lots of reasons, but one of them is that it's written using Spring Boot!
-   I'll be coming [to Washington DC with several of my cloudiest friends](http://www.meetup.com/DC-Cloud-Native-Meetup/events/226775603/?a=socialmedia) - join us!
-   There's a new checkbox on [the Spring Initializr](http://start.spring.io) to enable the [Ratpack](https://github.com/dsyer/spring-boot-ratpack) [Spring Boot integration](http://ratpack.io/manual/current/spring.html)!
-   There's also a new checkbox on [the Spring Initializr](http://start.spring.io) to enable [Activiti](https://spring.io/blog/2015/03/08/getting-started-with-activiti-and-spring-boot)
-   [Stephane Nicoll and Brian Clozel gave a great talk](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=7cemYpV1fNI) introducing Spring Boot at last week's Devoxx BE
-   I gave a talk introducing [Spring Cloud](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=SFDYdslOvu8) at the same show
-   and the amazing Oliver Gierke [gave a talk introducing the latest-and-greatest in Spring Data](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=NVB2AmMRUBI), also at Devoxx BE
-   our pal Matti Tahvonen, from Vaadin, has put together [a very cool post on using Spring Boot, Spring Data, etc., with Vaadin and Portlets](https://www.liferay.com/web/matti/blog/-/blogs/using-spring-data-jpa-with-a-liferay-portlet)
