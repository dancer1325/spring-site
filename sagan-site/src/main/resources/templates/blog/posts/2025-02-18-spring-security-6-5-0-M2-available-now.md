# Spring Security 6.5.0-M2 Available Now

_Releases | Josh Cummings |  February 18, 2025 | 0 Comments_

On behalf of the team and everyone who has contributed, I am pleased to announce the second milestone of the next Spring Security 6 minor release.

Among a number of feature enhancements, there are some that we'd love your attention on as we prepare them for general availability:

-   OAuth 2.0 Demonstrating Proof-of-Possession (DPoP) support - [#16574](https://github.com/spring-projects/spring-security/pull/16574)
-   Improvements to [One-Time Login](https://github.com/spring-projects/spring-security/pull/16297), [WebAuthn](https://github.com/spring-projects/spring-security/pull/16282), and [Method Security](https://github.com/spring-projects/spring-security/pull/16201)

Please check [the changelog](https://github.com/spring-projects/spring-framework/releases/tag/6.5.0-M2) for more details.
