# Spring AMQP 2.0 Release Candidate, 1.7.4 and 1.6.11 Are Available

_Releases | Gary Russell |  September 12, 2017 | 0 Comments_

I am pleased to announce that the [2.0.0.RC1](https://jira.spring.io/jira/secure/ReleaseNote.jspa?projectId=10450&version=16247) release candidate of Spring AMQP is now available in the [Spring milestone repository](https://repo.spring.io/milestone/).

This release adds some minor fixes/improvements since the final milestone [Milestone 5](https://spring.io/blog/2017/07/24/spring-amqp-2-0-0-milestone-5-is-available).

Thanks to all the community member for their feedback and contributions!

The GA release will follow shortly after the Spring Framework 5.0 GA release in September.

For a complete list of changes in 2.0, see [What’s New in the reference manual](http://docs.spring.io/spring-amqp/docs/2.0.0.RC1/reference/html/_introduction.html#_changes_in_2_0_since_1_7).

Maintenance releases [1.7.4](https://jira.spring.io/jira/secure/ReleaseNote.jspa?projectId=10450&version=16229) and [1.6.11](https://jira.spring.io/jira/secure/ReleaseNote.jspa?projectId=10450&version=16228) are also available now.
