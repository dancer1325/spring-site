# Spring Framework 5.3.6 and 5.2.14 available now

_Releases | Stéphane Nicoll |  April 13, 2021 | 0 Comments_

On behalf of the team and everyone who has contributed, I am pleased to announce that Spring Framework `5.3.6` and `5.2.14` are available now.

Spring Framework `5.3.6` includes [19 fixes and improvements](https://github.com/spring-projects/spring-framework/releases/tag/v5.3.6). Spring Framework `5.2.14` includes [11 selected fixes and improvements](https://github.com/spring-projects/spring-framework/releases/tag/v5.2.14.RELEASE).

Stay tuned for follow-up Spring Boot releases later this week.
