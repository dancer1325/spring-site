# This Week in Spring - 16 October, 2012

_Engineering | Josh Long |  October 17, 2012 | 0 Comments_

![](http://www.springsource.org/files/springone2012keynoteday1a.jpg)

Welcome to *This Week in Spring*, [SpringOne2GX 2012](http://www.springone2gx.com) edition! I'm putting this together from day 2 of the SpringOne2GX conference in Washington D.C.!

![](http://www.springsource.org/files/juergenkn.jpg)

Last night, we kicked off the event with a great keynote by [Juergen Hoeller](http://springone2gx.com/conference/washington/2012/10/speakers/juergen_hoeller) (pictured above), [Dr. Mark Pollack](http://springone2gx.com/conference/washington/2012/10/speakers/mark_pollack), [Graeme Rocher](http://springone2gx.com/conference/washington/2012/10/speakers/graeme_rocher), and [Adrian Colyer](http://springone2gx.com/conference/washington/2012/10/speakers/adrian_colyer), some amazing break dancing by international dancer *Agame*, and a *mysterious* kangaroo (see below)!

![](http://www.springsource.org/files/kangaroo.jpg) ![](http://www.springsource.org/files/prizes.jpg)

This is the premier event for JVM developers, featuring [great speakers from VMWare, SpringSource, TypeSafe, Neo4j, and many others](http://springone2gx.com/conference/washington/2012/10/schedule) speaking on all manner of topics. The week is just kicking off. While the conference is running, there's also going to be a contest to find the best applications built using SpringSource technologies (Spring, Grails) and deployed to [Cloud Foundry](http://www.cloudfoundry.org). I've seen the prizes (above), and I would enter too if I could!

![](http://www.springsource.org/files/CampaignBtn_Spring.png) ![](http://www.springsource.org/files/CampaignBtn_Grails.png) ![](http://www.springsource.org/files/CampaignBtn_CloudFoundry.png) ![](http://www.springsource.org/files/CampaignBtn_Groovy.png)
