# The SpringOne@Explore Calls for Paper is Open!

_News | Josh Long |  March 08, 2023 | 0 Comments_

Hi, Spring fans! The Spring community is everything. Without your voice, there is no Spring. And we love to hear from you. Make your voice heard and **[submit a talk](https://event.vmware.com/flow/vmware/explore2023lv/cfp/cfpHome)** for the **SpringOne@Explore** conference being held in tandem with [VMWare Explore](https://www.vmware.com/explore.html) in Las Vegas August 21st to 24th, 2023.

See you in Vegas!
