# Spring Batch 4.3.5 available now

_Releases | Mahmoud Ben Hassine |  February 23, 2022 | 0 Comments_

On behalf of the team and everyone who has contributed, I am pleased to announce that Spring Batch 4.3.5 has been released to Maven Central repository. This patch release comes with a number of bug fixes, enhancements and dependency updates. You can find the complete change logs here: [4.3.5](https://github.com/spring-projects/spring-batch/releases/tag/4.3.5).

I would like to thank all contributors who had a role in this release!
