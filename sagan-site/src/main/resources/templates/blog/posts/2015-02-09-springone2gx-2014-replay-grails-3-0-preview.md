# SpringOne2GX 2014 Replay: Grails 3.0 Preview

_News | Pieter Humphrey |  February 09, 2015 | 0 Comments_

Recorded at SpringOne2GX 2014.

Speaker: Graeme Rocher

Essential Grails Track

Slides: [http://www.slideshare.net/SpringCentral/2-gx-2014-grails-30-preview](http://www.slideshare.net/SpringCentral/2-gx-2014-grails-30-preview)

In this talk, Grails project lead Graeme Rocher will present a preview of the much anticipating version 3.0 rewrite of the Grails framework including presentation of the goals behind the changes and what will be achievable with the new version.
