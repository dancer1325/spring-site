Hi, Spring fans! What a crazy wonderful week it's been and what a wonderful week it'll be! It's the holidays. I've set my out-of-office status and am already winding down for the year. You can expect new episodes of *This Week in Spring* and a [*Bootiful Podcast* (@BootifulPodcast)](https://twitter.com/bootifulpodcast), of course, but I won't be doing any new [*Spring Tips* (@SpringTipsLive)](https://twitter.com/SpringTipsLive) videos until next year. That said, I can't wait for you to see what I'm cooking up for the new installmetn of *Spring Tips*! It's going to be a ton of fun!

Now then, we've got a crazy amount of stuff to get to this week. Let's dive right into it. It'll be Christmas before we talk again, so *Merry Christmas*, if you celebrate that. Otherwise, I just hope you're having a wonderful week. I look forward to next week's installment where of course we'll do our yearly *This Year in Spring* installment!
