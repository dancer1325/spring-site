# This Week in Spring: February 14th, 2012

_Engineering | Adam Fitzgerald |  February 15, 2012 | 0 Comments_

Welcome to another installment of *This Week in Spring*. Lot's of good content to go over, this week, so let's get to it.

Also: if you're in Stockholm, Sweden attending the [JFokus](http://www.jfokus.com) Java conference, be sure to stop by the VMWare booth to say hi or have your questions answered about [Spring](http://www.springsource.org), [Cloud Foundry](http://www.cloudfoundry.org), and [vFabric](http://www.vmware.com/products/application-platform/vfabric/overview.html).

1.  Alan Stewart has announced the [release of Spring Roo 1.2.1](http://blog.springsource.org/2012/02/10/spring-roo-1-2-1-release-available/), which includes fixes for multi-module Maven projects, *huge* performance enhancements, and updated support for PrimeFaces 3.1 and Google App Engine 1.6.2.
