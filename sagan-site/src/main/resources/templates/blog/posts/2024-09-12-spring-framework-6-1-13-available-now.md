# Spring Framework 6.1.13 Available Now

_Releases | Brian Clozel |  September 12, 2024 | 0 Comments_

On behalf of the team and everyone who has contributed, I am pleased to announce that Spring Framework `6.1.13` is available now.

Spring Framework `6.1.13` ships with [24 fixes and documentation improvements](https://github.com/spring-projects/spring-framework/releases/tag/v6.1.13). This version will be shipped with [Spring Boot 3.2.10](https://github.com/spring-projects/spring-boot/milestone/356) and [Spring Boot 3.3.4](https://github.com/spring-projects/spring-boot/milestone/357) next week.
