# Spring Security 2025-10 Releases

_Releases | Rob Winch |  October 20, 2025 | 0 Comments_

On behalf of the team and everyone who has contributed, I am pleased to announce the availability of Spring Security `7.0.0-RC1`, `6.4.12` and `6.5.6`.

The highlights of 7.0.0-RC1 include:

-   [Improved MFA Support](https://docs.spring.io/spring-security/reference/7.0/servlet/authentication/mfa.html)
-   [Support for providing credentials within a specified amount of recency](https://docs.spring.io/spring-security/reference/7.0/servlet/authentication/mfa.html#valid-duration)
-   [Jackson 3 Support](https://docs.spring.io/spring-security/reference/7.0/features/integrations/jackson.html)
-   [OAuth 2.0 Dynamic Client Registration Protocol](https://github.com/spring-projects/spring-security/issues/17964)

For a complete list of changes, refer to the changelogs:

-   [`7.0.0-RC1`](https://github.com/spring-projects/spring-security/releases/tag/7.0.0-RC1)
-   [`6.4.12`](https://github.com/spring-projects/spring-security/releases/tag/6.4.12)
-   [`6.5.6`](https://github.com/spring-projects/spring-security/releases/tag/6.5.6)
