# Spring Batch 4.2.5 and 4.3.1 available now

_Releases | Mahmoud Ben Hassine |  December 10, 2020 | 0 Comments_

I am pleased to announce that Spring Batch 4.2.5 and 4.3.1 have been released and are available now from Maven Central repository. These releases include a number of bug fixes and improvements. You can find the change logs here: [4.2.5](https://github.com/spring-projects/spring-batch/releases/tag/4.2.5.RELEASE) and [4.3.1](https://github.com/spring-projects/spring-batch/releases/tag/4.3.1).

I would like to thank all contributors who had a role in these releases!
