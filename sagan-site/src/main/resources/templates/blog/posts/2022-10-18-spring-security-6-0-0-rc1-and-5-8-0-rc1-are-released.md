# Spring Security 6.0.0-RC1 and 5.8.0-RC1 are released

_Releases | Steve Riesenberg |  October 18, 2022 | 0 Comments_

On behalf of the team and everyone who has contributed, I am pleased to announce that Spring Security 6.0.0-RC1 and 5.8.0-RC1 are available now.

See the [6.0.0-RC1](https://github.com/spring-projects/spring-security/releases/tag/6.0.0-RC1) and [5.8.0-RC1](https://github.com/spring-projects/spring-security/releases/tag/5.8.0-RC1) release notes for more details.
