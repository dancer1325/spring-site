# Spring Framework 6.2.14 Available Now

_Releases | Brian Clozel |  November 20, 2025 | 0 Comments_

On behalf of the team and everyone who has contributed, I am pleased to announce that Spring Framework `6.2.14` is available now.

Spring Framework `6.2.14` ships with [9 fixes and documentation improvements](https://github.com/spring-projects/spring-framework/releases/tag/v6.2.14). This version will be shipped today with Spring Boot `3.4.12` and `3.5.8`.
