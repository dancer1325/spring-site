# Spring for Apache Pulsar 1.1.12 and 1.2.6 are now available

_Releases | Chris Bono |  May 20, 2025 | 0 Comments_

On behalf of the team and everyone who has contributed, I’m happy to announce that Spring for Apache Pulsar `1.1.12` and `1.2.6` have been released and are now available from Maven Central.

The releases will be included in the upcoming Spring Boot [3.3.12](https://github.com/spring-projects/spring-boot/milestones/3.3.12) and [3.4.6](https://github.com/spring-projects/spring-boot/milestones/3.4.6) releases, respectively.

Please see the release notes ([1.1.12](https://github.com/spring-projects/spring-pulsar/releases/tag/v1.1.12) and [1.2.6](https://github.com/spring-projects/spring-pulsar/releases/tag/v1.2.6)) for more details.
