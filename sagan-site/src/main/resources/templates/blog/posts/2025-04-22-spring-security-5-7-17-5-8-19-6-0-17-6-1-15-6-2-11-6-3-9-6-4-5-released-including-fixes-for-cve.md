# Spring Security 5.7.17, 5.8.19, 6.0.17, 6.1.15, 6.2.11, 6.3.9, 6.4.5 Released, includes fix for CVE-2025-22234

_Releases | Joe Grandja |  April 22, 2025 | 1 Comment_

On behalf of the team and everyone who has contributed, I am pleased to announce that Spring Security `5.7.17`, `5.8.19`, `6.0.17`, `6.1.15`, `6.2.11`, `6.3.9`, and `6.4.5` are available now which fix [CVE-2025-22234](https://spring.io/security/cve-2025-22234).

Please refer to the [releases page](https://github.com/spring-projects/spring-security/releases) for more details.

Commercial customers using Spring Boot 2.7, 3.0, 3.1, or 3.2 will be able to update to Spring Boot 2.7.24.2, 3.0.19.2, 3.1.15.2, or 3.2.13.2 respectively to receive the corresponding Security releases 5.7.17, 6.0.17, 6.1.15, and 6.2.11. These Security versions are available now on the Spring commercial artifact repository and can be accessed with a [Spring Enterprise Subscription](https://enterprise.spring.io/).
