# SpringSource Seminar Day Linz in Review

_Engineering | Juergen Hoeller |  September 23, 2008 | 0 Comments_

A brief pictorial review of the SpringSource Seminar Day in Linz, having happened on September 8th, 2008, at the Bergschloessl Linz... More than 150 people were listening to a six-pack of presentations about what's new and upcoming at SpringSource. The "Story of Spring" keynote by Rod Johnson and Adrian Colyer was a great start into a day full of information: about the SpringSource Application Platform, the SpringSource Tool Suite, Spring 3.0, etc. (See the [original blog announcement](http://blog.springsource.com/2008/06/29/springsource-seminar-day-in-central-europe/) for details on the agenda.) ![](http://blog.springsource.com/wp-content/uploads/2008/09/seminarday_linz_2008_013.jpg "A seminar room full of SpringOne shirts") ![](http://blog.springsource.com/wp-content/uploads/2008/09/seminarday_linz_2008_198.jpg "A seminar room full of people paying attention") ![](http://blog.springsource.com/wp-content/uploads/2008/09/seminarday_linz_2008_096.jpg "SpringSource staff not fitting into the seminar room anymore") ![](http://blog.springsource.com/wp-content/uploads/2008/09/seminarday_linz_2008_157.jpg "People eagerly grabbing lunch") ![](http://blog.springsource.com/wp-content/uploads/2008/09/seminarday_linz_2008_091.jpg "Nice people to talk to at the SpringSource booth") ![](http://blog.springsource.com/wp-content/uploads/2008/09/seminarday_linz_2008_201.jpg "People enjoying a break outside")

It was a pleasure to see so many people attending: from Austria as well as from Germany and Eastern Europe - and even from Norway! I hope you enjoyed the seminar and your stay in Upper Austria. Looking forward to seeing you again at the [SpringOne Europe 2009](http://www.springone.com/) conference in Amsterdam, April 27-29... as well as at upcoming [EJUG Austria](http://www.ejug.at/) events!

We are also finally able to provide the slide decks from the seminar as a PDF file archive, which you can download from [here](http://s3.springsource.com.s3.amazonaws.com/CONF/SpringSourceSeminarDayLinz.zip). Enjoy!

Cheers,

Juergen
