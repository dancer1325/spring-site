# Spring Security 6.5.0 Is Out!

_Releases | Josh Cummings |  May 19, 2025 | 1 Comment_

On behalf of the team and everyone who has contributed, I am pleased to announce the general availability of the final Spring Security 6 minor release, 6.5.0!

To see all the new features, please take a look at [What's New in Spring Security 6.5](https://docs.spring.io/spring-security/reference/6.5-SNAPSHOT/whats-new.html) in the reference.

Also, since this is the target release for migrating from 6.x to 7.x, please also begin reviewing [the migration guide](https://docs.spring.io/spring-security/reference/6.5-SNAPSHOT/migration-7/index.html).

Please check [the release changelog](https://github.com/spring-projects/spring-security/releases/tag/6.5.0) as well as the previous changelogs for [6.5.0-M1](https://github.com/spring-projects/spring-security/releases/tag/6.5.0), [6.5.0-M2](https://github.com/spring-projects/spring-security/releases/tag/6.5.0-M2), [6.5.0-M3](https://github.com/spring-projects/spring-security/releases/tag/6.5.0-M3), and [6.5.0-RC1](https://github.com/spring-projects/spring-security/releases/tag/6.5.0-RC1) for more details.
