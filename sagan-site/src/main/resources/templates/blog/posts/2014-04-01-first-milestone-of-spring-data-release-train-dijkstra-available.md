# First Milestone of Spring Data Release Train Dijkstra Available

_Releases | Oliver Drotbohm |  April 01, 2014 | 0 Comments_

I am excited to announce the availability of the first milestone of the [Dijkstra](http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Edsger_W._Dijkstra) release train of the Spring Data umbrella project. This train iteration is a very special one as we have 5 new modules joining the release train: Spring Data [Couchbase](http://projects.spring.io/spring-data-couchbase/), [Cassandra](http://projects.spring.io/spring-data-cassandra/), [Elasticsearch](https://github.com/spring-projects/spring-data-elasticsearch), [Gemfire](http://projects.spring.io/spring-data-gemfire/) and [Redis](http://projects.spring.io/spring-data-redis/). I am even more excited about that as the majority of them are community lead projects. These are the modules included:

The team around these 12 modules squashed 169 tickets for this milestone and fixed 58 further bugs that will also be backported to the Codd services releases. See this [JIRA query](https://jira.spring.io/issues/?filter=14544) for a full list of changes.

We've prepared a [curated changelog](https://github.com/spring-projects/spring-data-commons/wiki/Release-Train-Dijkstra) in our wiki for you to get an overview over what is included in the first milestone. We'll have additional blog posts coming up to present some selected new features in more detail. Until then, we're looking forward to your feedback in [JIRA](http://jira.spring.io) via Twitter or on [StackOverflow](http://www.stackoverflow.com).
