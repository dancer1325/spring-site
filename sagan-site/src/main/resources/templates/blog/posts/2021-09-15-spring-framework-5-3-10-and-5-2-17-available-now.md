# Spring Framework 5.3.10 and 5.2.17 available now

_Releases | Stéphane Nicoll |  September 15, 2021 | 2 Comments_

On behalf of the team and everyone who has contributed, I am pleased to announce that Spring Framework `5.3.10` and `5.2.17` are available now.

Spring Framework `5.3.10` includes [45 fixes and improvements](https://github.com/spring-projects/spring-framework/releases/tag/v5.3.10). Spring Framework `5.2.17` includes [13 selected fixes and improvements](https://github.com/spring-projects/spring-framework/releases/tag/v5.2.17.RELEASE).

Stay tuned for follow-up Spring Boot releases next week.
