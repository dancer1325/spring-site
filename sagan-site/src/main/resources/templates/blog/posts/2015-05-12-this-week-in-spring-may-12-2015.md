# This Week in Spring - May 12, 2015

_Engineering | Josh Long |  May 12, 2015 | 0 Comments_

Welcome to another installment of *This Week in Spring*! This week, I'm in Chicago, IL, for [the spectacular GOTO conference, Chicago edition](http://gotocon.com/chicago-2015/). [If you're in the Chicago area, ping me on Twitter (@starbuxman)](http://twitter.com/starbuxman). Cloud Foundry Summit just wrapped today as 1500 people came together to talk about the community and the technology. Spring made a [strong showing](https://spring.io/blog/2015/04/14/spring-at-cloud-foundry-summit-may-11-12-2015), even getting a general session shoutout from James Watters for [Spring Boot passing 1 Million downloads](https://twitter.com/rohit_ghai/status/598269938021740544) in April.

Now, without further ado..

-   Spring Tool Suite ninja Kris De Volder [just announced the new support for editing `.yml` files in STS](http://spring.io/blog/2015/05/11/new-in-sts-3-7-0-spring-boot-yaml-editor) - this is particularly awesome if you're using Spring Boot!
-   Spring IO lead and all-around mad-scientist Andy Wilkinson just [released the Gradle dependency management plugin 0.5.1.RELEASE](http://spring.io/blog/2015/05/11/gradle-dependency-management-plugin-0-5-1-released). This plugin brings the power of Maven's `dependencyManagement` mechanism to Gradle and makes it easy to create consistent builds.
-   Spring Integration lead [Gary Russell just released Spring AMQP 1.4.5 and 1.5.0-M1](http://spring.io/blog/2015/05/08/spring-amqp-1-4-5-release-and-1-5-0-m1-available). The 1.5 series introduces class-level `@RabbitListener` annotations (not just method-level ones), auto-declared queues and bindings, configurable exchange/routing Key for replies, a `RabbitManagementTemplate` which uses RabbitMQ's REST API bindings, not the hitherto supported Erlang-specific integrations.
-   Did you get a chance to see my webinar on smarter service-to-service invocations with Spring Cloud? If not, [check it out, it's online](http://spring.io/blog/2015/05/07/webinar-replay-smarter-service-to-service-invocations-with-spring-cloud).
-   Almost out of SpringOne2GX replays, this week's are out! Check out Owen Rubel talk about [API Abstraction and API Chaining in Grails](https://spring.io/blog/2015/05/12/springone2gx-2014-replay-api-abstraction-and-api-chaining-in-grails), Fabrice Matrat as he [Dives into Single-Page Application with RaveJS and Grails](https://spring.io/blog/2015/05/12/springone2gx-2014-replay-dive-into-single-page-application-with-ravejs-and-grails), Hans Dockter gives you some roadmap on [The Future of Gradle - The Ultimate Build System](https://spring.io/blog/2015/05/12/springone2gx-2014-replay-the-future-of-gradle-the-ultimate-build-system) and our friends at JFrog Baruch Sadogursky and Frederic Simon talk about [Writing AST Transformations - Get Practical in 90 minutes](https://spring.io/blog/2015/05/12/springone2gx-2014-replays-writing-ast-transformations-get-practical-in-90-minutes)
-   This following blog has very little to do with Spring, but I think it's super important. It's a post I put together that is meant to help the wayward student taking her or his first steps in computer science. I hope it's useful [and would love feedback, if you've got something to add](http://spring.io/blog/2015/05/11/to-my-fellow-students).
-   Turkcell's Erdem Gunay, and our pal, is giving a webinar on June 9th explaining [how Spring is used in Turkcell's personal cloud service](http://spring.io/blog/2015/05/06/webinar-a-comprehensive-spring-showcase-personal-cloud-storage-application).
-   The Madrid Groovy User Group publishes their videos and the recent Spanish-language talk, by Pablo Alba, [introduces websockets and Spring Boot](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=eVox8LBS2RE).
-   Niels Bech Nielsen's talk on Spring Boot [for microservices looks pretty interesting](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=W-TXm3oQ9yg)
-   [Spring Data ninja Thomas Darimont's German-language talk on Spring XD](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=QduW8RgKV3o) is up and it looks mighty interesting! (Even if I don't particularly understand the language itself..)
-   Spring Data lead Oliver Gierke's talk on [Spring Cloud for the MicroExchange conference is also now up and available](http://www.infoq.com/presentations/spring-cloud)
-   Daniel Maas put together a nice blog on [intelligent microservice metrics with Spring Boot and Statsd](https://objectpartners.com/2015/05/07/intelligent-microservice-metrics-with-spring-boot-and-statsd/)
