# This Week in Spring - January 8th, 2019

_Engineering | Josh Long |  January 08, 2019 | 2 Comments_

Hi Spring fans! Welcome to another installment of *This Week in Spring*! This week I've just concluded the second of my two online trainings, one on Monday on [*Reactive Spring*](http://twitter.com/ReactiveSpring), and another today on using [Spring Boot in Kotlin](https://www.safaribooksonline.com/live-training/courses/reactive-spring-boot/0636920183075/). This week I'll be in Montreal, Canada for an appearance at the [Montreal JUG](https://www.meetup.com/montreal-jug/events/257561730/) and at [Dawscon](https://www.dawsoncollege.qc.ca/dawscon/). I'd love to see you at both events! Come out and say "Hi!" or "Bonjour!"
