# This Week in Spring - May 3rd, 2022

_Engineering | Josh Long |  May 03, 2022 | 0 Comments_

Hi, Spring fans! Welcome to another installment of *This Week in Spring*! How are you doin'?

I'm excited! This week I'm speaking at the [ArabJUG](https://twitter.com/arabjug), and I'll be speaking at Microsoft's *huuuge* [JDConf](https://docs.microsoft.com/en-us/events/learntv/jdconf-2022/) event. Both of these are virtual. Then, next Monday, I'm on a plane bound for London, UK, where I'll be speaking [at Devoxx UK 2022](https://www.devoxx.co.uk/). Then, not even two weeks later, I'll be speaking at [Spring IO](https://2022.springio.net/), in Barcelona, Spain! Then a week later, I'll be speaking at [JNation](https://2022.jnation.pt/), in Lisbon, Portugal. To say that I am excited would be an understatement, my friends.

And all of that ignores the great stuff since *last* week. Let's dive right into it!
