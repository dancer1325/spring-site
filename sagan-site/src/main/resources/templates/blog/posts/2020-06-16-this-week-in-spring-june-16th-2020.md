I've got a few upcoming speaking engagements you might be interested in. [I'll be speaking (virtually) at the San Francisco JUG on June 24th at 6:30 PM](https://twitter.com/pieterhumphrey/status/1272331813445185536?s=12). Join me!

You don't want to miss this!
