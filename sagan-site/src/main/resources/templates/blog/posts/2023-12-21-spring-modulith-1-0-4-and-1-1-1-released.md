# Spring Modulith 1.0.4 and 1.1.1 released

_Releases | Oliver Drotbohm |  December 21, 2023 | 0 Comments_

On behalf of the community I am happy to announce Spring Modulith 1.0.4 and 1.1.1. The releases primarily contain bug fixes and dependency upgrades to the latest Spring Boot versions.

Find more information in the corresponding change logs of [1.0.4](https://github.com/spring-projects/spring-modulith/releases/tag/1.0.4) and [1.1.1](https://github.com/spring-projects/spring-modulith/releases/tag/1.1.1).

Happy holidays!
