# Spring Security 5.1.2, 5.0.10, 4.2.10  Released

_Releases | Rob Winch |  November 29, 2018 | 1 Comment_

On behalf of the community I am pleased to announce the release of Spring Security 5.1.2 ([changelog](https://github.com/spring-projects/spring-security/issues?q=is%3Aclosed+milestone%3A5.1.2+sort%3Aupdated-desc)), 5.0.10 ([changelog](https://github.com/spring-projects/spring-security/issues?q=is%3Aclosed+milestone%3A5.0.10+sort%3Aupdated-desc)), and 4.2.10 ([changelog](https://github.com/spring-projects/spring-security/issues?q=is%3Aclosed+milestone%3A4.2.10+sort%3Aupdated-desc)). This series of releases provides a round of bug fixes and users are encouraged to update to the latest patch release.
