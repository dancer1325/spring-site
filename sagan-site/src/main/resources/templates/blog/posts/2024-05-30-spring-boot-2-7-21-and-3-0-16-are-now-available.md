# Spring Boot 2.7.21 and 3.0.16 are now available

_Releases | Andy Wilkinson |  May 30, 2024 | 0 Comments_

On behalf of the team, I am pleased to announce that Spring Boot 2.7.21 and 3.0.16 have been released for [support](https://tanzu.vmware.com/spring) customers. The releases are available from [https://packages.broadcom.com](https://packages.broadcom.com).

-   Spring Boot [2.7.21](https://enterprise.spring.io/projects/spring-boot/changelog/2.7.21) ships with 17 bug fixes and dependency upgrades.
-   Spring Boot [3.0.16](https://enterprise.spring.io/projects/spring-boot/changelog/3.0.16) ships with 40 bug fixes and dependency upgrades.

The next 2.7.x and 3.0.x releases for support customers are planned for 3 months' time.
