# This Week in Spring - June 27th, 2023

_Engineering | Josh Long |  June 27, 2023 | 0 Comments_

Hi Spring fans! Welcome to another installment of *This Week in Spring*! This week I am in Seoul talking to developers about the latest-and-greatest in Spring Boot 3! There's so much great stuff coming, and so much great stuff already. There are a few things I'm super excited about.

First, yesterday I did a joint presentation with my friend the one the only the amazing Trustin Lee, creator of Netty and lead of the Armeria Project over at LINE corporation. LINE is a super popular social messaging application used a lot in Korea and in Japan. It was a ton of [fun collaborating with, and copresenting with, him](https://pbs.twimg.com/media/FznQPxgWYAAMd0D?format=jpg&name=large).

Next, I'm elated that I'm going to be presenting at the **Seoul SpringOne Tour** on June 28th (tomorrow!), 2023! Come join me [and we'll look at Spring Boot 3!](https://connect.tanzu.vmware.com/Event_Spring_One_Tour_Q224_KR.html).

Relatedly, I'll also be doing a SpringOne Tour event in Singapore on July 4th at 09:30! Come join us and we'll see [how Spring Boot 3.1 and TAP take you to production faster](https://connect.tanzu.vmware.com/SpringOne_Tour_SG_July.html)!
