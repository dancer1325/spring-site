# Spring Shell 2.1.8, 3.0.2 and 3.1.0-M2 are now available

_Releases | Janne Valkealahti |  April 17, 2023 | 0 Comments_

On behalf of the team and everyone who has contributed, I'm happy to announce that Spring Shell `2.1.8`, `3.0.2` and `3.1.0-M2` has been released and is now available from Maven Central and [https://repo.spring.io/milestone](https://repo.spring.io/milestone) respectively.

Please see the [release notes 2.1.8](https://github.com/spring-projects/spring-shell/releases/tag/v2.1.8), [release notes 3.0.2](https://github.com/spring-projects/spring-shell/releases/tag/v3.0.2) and [release notes 3.1.0-M2](https://github.com/spring-projects/spring-shell/releases/tag/v3.1.0-M2) for more details.

Thanks to all those who have contributed with issue reports and pull requests.
