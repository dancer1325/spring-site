# Spring Security 5.5 goes GA

_Releases | Eleftheria Stein-Kousathana |  May 18, 2021 | 2 Comments_

On behalf of the community, it is my pleasure to announce the general availability of Spring Security 5.5. This release is the result of the work that went into [5.5.0-M1](https://github.com/spring-projects/spring-security/releases/tag/5.5.0-M1), [5.5.0-M2](https://github.com/spring-projects/spring-security/releases/tag/5.5.0-M2), [5.5.0-M3](https://github.com/spring-projects/spring-security/releases/tag/5.5.0-M3), [5.5.0-RC1](https://github.com/spring-projects/spring-security/releases/tag/5.5.0-RC1), [5.5.0-RC2](https://github.com/spring-projects/spring-security/releases/tag/5.5.0-RC2) and [5.5.0](https://github.com/spring-projects/spring-security/releases/tag/5.5.0). In combination, they close 250+ tickets.

You can find the highlights of 5.5 in the [What’s new](https://docs.spring.io/spring-security/site/docs/5.5.0/reference/html5/#new) section of the Spring Security reference.

As always, we look forward to hearing your [feedback](https://github.com/spring-projects/spring-security/issues)!
