_Engineering | Josh Long |  August 03, 2023 | 0 Comments_

Hi, Spring fans! I just crossed 13 years on the Spring team! I just got to spend a lovely day in the presence of one of my heroes and friends, [UL Systems](https://www.ulsystems.co.jp) founder and chairman, [Shigeru Urushibara-san (@ulsystems)](https://twitter.com/ulsystems), here in Tokyo, Japan, and in this episode, we sort of flip the script. We had a brief discussion where he interviewed *me*. If you want a great discussion where there's a ton to learn... then you might want to check out this episode where [I interviewed him](https://bootifulpodcast.fm/#/episodes/c9ffbd9e-1d62-4b62-9c55-10f64d09dbdc) in 2021! Otherwise, listen on. Thank you, Urushibara-san! And thank you, everyone. Here's to another amazing 13 years!
