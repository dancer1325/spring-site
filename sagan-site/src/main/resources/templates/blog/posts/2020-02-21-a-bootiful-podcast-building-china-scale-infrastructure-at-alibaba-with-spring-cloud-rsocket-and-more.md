# A Bootiful Podcast: Building China-scale Infrastructure at Alibaba with Spring Cloud, Rsocket, and more

_Engineering | Josh Long |  February 21, 2020 | 2 Comments_

Hi, Spring fans! In this installment Josh Long ([@starbuxman](http://twitter.com/starbuxman)) talks to friends - Mercy Ma (马昕曦), Andy Shi (施孜海), and Jim Fang (方剑) - from Alibaba. These engineers work on Spring Cloud Alibaba, some of the open source infrastructure coming out of Alibaba designed to support Spring applications running at Alibaba, and more.
