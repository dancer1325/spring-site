# Spring for Apache Pulsar 1.1.9 and 1.2.3 are now available

_Releases | Chris Bono |  February 18, 2025 | 0 Comments_

On behalf of the team and everyone who has contributed, I’m happy to announce that Spring for Apache Pulsar `1.1.9` and `1.2.3` have been released and are now available from Maven Central.

The releases will be included in the upcoming Spring Boot [3.3.9](https://github.com/spring-projects/spring-boot/milestones/3.3.9) and [3.4.3](https://github.com/spring-projects/spring-boot/milestones/3.4.3) releases, respectively.

Please see the release notes ([1.1.9](https://github.com/spring-projects/spring-pulsar/releases/tag/v1.1.9) and [1.2.3](https://github.com/spring-projects/spring-pulsar/releases/tag/v1.2.3)) for more details.
