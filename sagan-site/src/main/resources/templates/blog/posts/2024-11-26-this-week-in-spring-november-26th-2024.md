Hi, Spring fans! Welcome to another installment of *This Week in Spring*!

Happy Spring Boot 3.4 release month to those who celebrate! And, also, Happy Thanksgiving to those who celebrate! Spring Boot 3.4 brings with it long-anticipated updates to the entire portfolio of Spring projects, and so a lot of the content in this roundup has to do with those releases!

-   Starting with an interview with Spring Framework lead Juergen Hoeller [and Spring Framework contributor Sébastien Deleuze over on InfoQ](https://www.infoq.com/news/2024/11/spring-6-2-spring-boot-3-4/)

So I wrote them all down and published them as a series of blogs looking at Spring Framework, Spring Data, Spring Batch, Spring Integration, Spring Security, Spring Modulith, and Spring AI.
