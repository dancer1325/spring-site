# This Week in Spring - August 12th, 2025

_Engineering | Josh Long |  August 12, 2025 | 1 Comment_

Hi, Spring fans! Welcome to another installment of *This Week in Spring*! It's August 5th! Which means we're only a few short weeks away until SpringOne 2025! [Have you registered?](https://springone.io) There's so much to cover this week, so let's dive right into it!

-   I love Spring Modulith and I love JetBrains IntelliJ IDEA! So imagine my joy to discover that the new IntelliJ IDEA release [features support for Spring Modulith!](https://youtu.be/fGExm_Rlees?si=BUG6RY_1gyE-6rtB)
-   [Spring Cloud Oakwood has been released](https://spring.io/blog/2025/07/31/spring-cloud-2025-1-0-M1-aka-oakwood-has-been-released)
-   In last week's installment of *A Bootiful Podcast*, I talked to [Spring Security lead and legend Rob Winch](https://spring.io/blog/2025/07/31/a-bootiful-podcast-rob-winch) about the new and novel in Spring Security and what we're going to be talking about at SpringOne 2025! (Have [you registered yet](https://springone.io)?)
-   [Spring Shell's been updated!](https://spring.io/blog/2025/08/04/spring-shell-3-4-1-available)
-   [Spring AI has been updated](https://spring.io/blog/2025/08/08/spring-ai-1)
-   [Spring gRPC 0.10.0 is available!](https://spring.io/blog/2025/08/11/spring-grpc-0-10-0-available-now) This is another up-and-coming project that I've been watching with not just a little excitement, because it marries the amazing programming model of Spring and Spring Boot with the awesome gRPC protocol.
-   Have you played with [Spring's service loader facility?](https://medium.com/@umeshcapg/the-spring-pattern-that-impressed-my-manager-and-dumps-if-else-ba29e0b80ad5)
-   I loved Sébastien Deleuze's [talk introducing Kotlin support in Spring Boot 4](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=Ip1IFdlNPIY)
-   Check out the latest and greatest in [IntelliJ - lots of good stuff for Spring developers](https://www.jetbrains.com/idea/whatsnew/)!
-   [IBM has a page on Spring Boot?](https://www.google.com/url?sa=t&source=web&rct=j&opi=89978449&url=https://www.ibm.com/think/topics/java-spring-boot&ved=2ahUKEwiXmeiB7ISPAxVukWoFHdWgOqUQFnoECBcQAQ&usg=AOvVaw3uYB9nXHKK-AGf0hylkEeP)
-   This is a pretty good roadmap for getting [started with backend service development in terms of Spring.](https://www.geeksforgeeks.org/springboot/best-way-to-master-spring-boot-a-complete-roadmap/). I'd add Spring AI and Spring Modulith to the mix, too.
