# Spring Shell 2.1.14, 3.0.9, 3.1.5 and 3.2.0-M3 are now available

_Releases | Janne Valkealahti |  November 08, 2023 | 0 Comments_

On behalf of the team and everyone who has contributed, I'm happy to announce that Spring Shell `2.1.14`, `3.0.9`, `3.1.5` and `3.2.0-M3` has been released and are now available from Maven Central and Spring Repos respectively.

Please see the [release notes 2.1.14](https://github.com/spring-projects/spring-shell/releases/tag/v2.1.14), [release notes 3.0.9](https://github.com/spring-projects/spring-shell/releases/tag/v3.0.9), [release notes 3.1.5](https://github.com/spring-projects/spring-shell/releases/tag/v3.1.5) and [release notes 3.2.0-M3](https://github.com/spring-projects/spring-shell/releases/tag/v3.2.0-M3) for more details.

Thanks to all those who have contributed with issue reports and pull requests.
