# Spring Vault 3.0.3 available

_Releases | Mark Paluch |  June 19, 2023 | 0 Comments_

On behalf of the team and everyone who has contributed, I am pleased to announce that the Spring Vault `3.0.3` release is available now.

Spring Vault `3.0.3` ships with [12 fixes and selected improvements](https://github.com/spring-projects/spring-vault/releases/tag/3.0.3).
