# Spring Modulith 1.3.1, 1.2.7, and 1.1.12 released

_Releases | Oliver Drotbohm |  December 20, 2024 | 0 Comments_

On behalf of the community, I am delighted to announce the releases of Spring Modulith [1.3.1](https://github.com/spring-projects/spring-modulith/releases/tag/1.3.1), [1.2.7](https://github.com/spring-projects/spring-modulith/releases/tag/1.2.7), and [1.1.12](https://github.com/spring-projects/spring-modulith/releases/tag/1.1.12).

The releases primarily ship dependency updates and bug fixes, but 1.3.1 also includes a couple of improvements in both our own [reference documentation](https://github.com/spring-projects/spring-modulith/issues?q=is%3Aissue+label%3A%22in%3A+reference+documentation%22+milestone%3A%221.3.1%22+) but also in our [documentation generation support](https://github.com/spring-projects/spring-modulith/issues?q=is%3Aissue+label%3A%22in%3A+documentation+support%22+milestone%3A%221.3.1%22+).

Find more information in the full change logs of the individual releases linked above.

Happy Holidays!
