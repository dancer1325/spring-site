# Spring Initializr 0.9.1 available now

_Releases | Stéphane Nicoll |  August 17, 2020 | 0 Comments_

On behalf of the team and everyone who has contributed, I’m happy to announce that Spring Initializr 0.9.1 has been released and is now available from repo.spring.io and Maven Central.

This release includes [2 fixes](https://github.com/spring-io/initializr/releases/tag/v0.9.1). Thanks to all those who have contributed with issue reports and pull requests.
