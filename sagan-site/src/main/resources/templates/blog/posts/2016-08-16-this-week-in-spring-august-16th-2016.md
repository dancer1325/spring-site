# This Week in Spring - August 16th, 2016

_Engineering | Josh Long |  August 16, 2016 | 0 Comments_

Welcome to another installment of *This Week in Spring*! Since we last spoke I've presented at conferences and to customers in London, Beijing, Shanghai and Singapore - where I am now. Tomorrow, Wednesday, I'll be speaking [at the Singapore Spring Meetup](http://www.meetup.com/singasug/events/233028592/) - join me! It's been quite a few days!

-   Spring Data ninja Mark Paluch has done another [great post looking at Spring Cloud Vault](https://spring.io/blog/2016/08/15/managing-your-database-secrets-with-vault)
-   Spring Security lead Rob Winch [just announced Spring Security 4.1.2](https://spring.io/blog/2016/08/12/spring-security-4-1-2-released)
-   Spring Integration ninja Artem Bilan just announced [Spring for Apache Kafka 1.0.3](https://spring.io/blog/2016/08/12/spring-for-apache-kafka-1-0-3-available-now)
-   Check out this deck to [learn about how Spring Boot is used at PayPal](http://www.slideshare.net/SpringCentral/spring-boot-paypal)
-   I liked my buddy Richard Seroter's review [of our SpringOne Platform event a few weeks ago](https://seroter.wordpress.com/2016/08/09/enterprises-fighting-back-spring-boot-is-the-best-and-other-springone-platform-takeaways/)
-   I loved Camille Fournier's post on some real-life architecture patterns of microservices and, particularly, her observations on the challenges of [a monorepo or a monolithic application and how it relates to human dynamics](https://medium.com/@skamille/microservices-real-architectural-patterns-68bd83bbb6cd#.hkk55wlwk)
-   Mitsuyuki Shiiba, on the Hatena blog, put together a brief [and essential look at Pivotal's Reactor project](http://bufferings.hatenablog.com/entry/2016/08/09/073802)
-   I'm seeing this a lot in the work I'm doing in the last few years, and thought this post was interesting: [why businesses are rediscovering PaaS](http://www.information-age.com/technology/cloud-and-virtualisation/123461817/why-businesses-have-reignited-search-paas-field-dreams)
-   Some cool videos going up recently from the Cloud Foundry foundation, including this, [Does Cloud Foundry Support Containers](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=Ue_ArLRs00E&feature=youtu.be), and this, [Grape Up Revisits Cloud Foundry: UDP, VoIP, and Beyond!](https://www.cloudfoundry.org/grape-up-revisits-cloud-foundry-udp-voip-and-beyond/)
-   I liked Rafael Salerno's blog on [understanding service registration and discovery with Spring Cloud](http://salerno-rafael.blogspot.sg/2016/08/spring-cloud-netflix-how-works-service.html)
