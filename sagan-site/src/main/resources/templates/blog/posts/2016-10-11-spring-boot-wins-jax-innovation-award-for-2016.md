# Spring Boot wins JAX Innovation Award for 2016

_News | Pieter Humphrey |  October 11, 2016 | 2 Comments_

The winners of the [JAX Innovation Awards 2016](https://jaxenter.com/winners-jax-innovation-awards-2016-jax-london-129588.html) have been announced this evening at the JAX Innovation Awards special event in London, United Kingdom. The winner of the ‘Most innovative contribution to the Java ecosystem’ category is [Spring Boot](http://projects.spring.io/spring-boot). Docker has been voted the most innovative solution to software delivery and DevOps while the Special Jury Award went to Let’s Encrypt.
