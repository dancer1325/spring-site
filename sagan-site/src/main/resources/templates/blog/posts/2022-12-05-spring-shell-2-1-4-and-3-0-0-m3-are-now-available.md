# Spring Shell 2.1.4 and 3.0.0-M3 are now available

_Releases | Janne Valkealahti |  December 05, 2022 | 0 Comments_

On behalf of the team and everyone who has contributed, I'm happy to announce that Spring Shell `2.1.4` and `3.0.0-M3` has been released and is now available from [https://repo.spring.io/release](https://repo.spring.io/release), Maven Central and [https://repo.spring.io/milestone](https://repo.spring.io/milestone) respectively.

`3.0.0-M3` is first milestone which builds on Spring Boot 3.0.0 GA version.

Please see the [release notes 2.1.4](https://github.com/spring-projects/spring-shell/releases/tag/v2.1.4) and [release notes 3.0.0-M3](https://github.com/spring-projects/spring-shell/releases/tag/v3.0.0-M3) for more details.

Thanks to all those who have contributed with issue reports and pull requests.
