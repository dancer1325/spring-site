# Spring Framework 5.2 RC2 available now

_Releases | Stéphane Nicoll |  September 05, 2019 | 2 Comments_

On behalf of the team and everyone who has contributed, I am pleased to announce that the second release candidate of Spring Framework 5.2 has been released and is available from our [milestone repository](https://repo.spring.io/milestone).

Spring Framework `5.2.0.RC2` includes [63 fixes and improvements](https://github.com/spring-projects/spring-framework/releases/tag/v5.2.0.RC2) and is our last stop before GA proper scheduled 3 weeks from now.

Next up is Spring Boot `2.2.0.M6`, stay tuned!
