# A Bootiful Podcast: Lightbend CTO Jonas Bonér on his epic, aspect-oriented, distributed, reactive work

_Engineering | Josh Long |  September 17, 2020 | 0 Comments_

Hi, Spring fans! In this episode, [Josh Long (@starbuxman)](http://twitter.com/starbuxman) talks to his friend and Lightbend CTO [Jonas Bonér (@jboner)](http://twitter.com/jboner) about his epic, aspect-oriented, distributed, and reactive work over the decades, about the cooperation with the Spring team on the [Reactive Streams](http://reactive-streams.io/), the [Reactive Foundation](http://Reactive.foundation), and more.

-   Check out the [upcoming Reactive Summit](https://www.reactivesummit.org/)! Both Jonas and Josh will be speaking there :-)
