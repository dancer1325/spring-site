# Spring Batch 4.2.7 and 4.3.3 are out!

_Releases | Mahmoud Ben Hassine |  May 19, 2021 | 3 Comments_

On behalf of the team and everyone who contributed, I am pleased to announce that Spring Batch 4.2.7 and 4.3.3 have been released and are available now from Maven Central repository. These patch releases come with a number of bug fixes, improvements and dependency updates. You can find the complete change logs here: [4.2.7](https://github.com/spring-projects/spring-batch/releases/tag/4.2.7.RELEASE) and [4.3.3](https://github.com/spring-projects/spring-batch/releases/tag/4.3.3).

I would like to thank all contributors who had a role in these releases!
