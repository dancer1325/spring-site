# This Week in Spring - May 10th, 2022

_Engineering | Josh Long |  May 10, 2022 | 0 Comments_

Hi, Spring fans! I'm writing this from - I can't believe I get to say this - *abroad*! I'm in London, UK! Now, this is not particularly noteworthy for those millions who already live here. But I *don't* live here. I'm a visitor! I live in San Francisco. I had to *fly* here! On a *plane*! With other people! *ACROSS THE OCEAN*. This is my first international flight since March of 2020, and I couldn't be more excited to be here for Devoxx UK and also just to catch up with old friends I haven't seen in nearly three years. If you know me, and how I [used to travel](https://joshlong.com/jl/blogPost/2019_year_in_review.html), you'll appreciate how odd it is for me to be this excited to have crossed a border. It's been *sooo* long! I've missed the process. I've missed seeing people. I've missed all of it.

One thing I haven't missed is a lot of this week's new and noteworthy, which I recap in this week's *This Week in Spring* roundup. Let's goooo!
