# This Week in Spring - August 5th, 2025

_Engineering | Josh Long |  August 05, 2025 | 1 Comment_

Hi, Spring fans! Welcome to another installment of *This Week in Spring*! It's August 5th! Which means we're only 20 days away until SpringOne 2025! [Have you registered?](https://springone.io) There's so much to cover this week, so let's dive right into it!

-   [Spring Shell 3.4.1 is out!](https://spring.io/blog/2025/08/04/spring-shell-3-4-1-available) - the new release includes a number of bug fixes, dependency updates, and refinements of the documentation.
-   In last week's installment of *A Bootiful Podcast*, I talked to Spring Security lead about what to expect [at SpringOne 2025, and looked ahead at the upcoming Spring Security 7.0 release.](https://spring.io/blog/2025/07/31/a-bootiful-podcast-rob-winch)
-   [Spring Cloud 2025.1.0-M1 (aka Oakwood) has been released](https://spring.io/blog/2025/07/31/spring-cloud-2025-1-0-M1-aka-oakwood-has-been-released)
-   have you checked out the incredible new features in [IntelliJ IDEA 2025.2](https://www.jetbrains.com/idea/whatsnew/)? One of my favorite new features is that the IDE surfaces Spring Modulith validation front-and-center in the IDE itself! I've been waiting for this one for a *while*, and I am so happy it's here!
-   I loved this feature so much, in fact, that I put together a new video looking at its use in IntelliJ IDEA 2025.2 in [terms of a Spring Modulith demo - check it out!](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=fGExm_Rlees)
-   I looked at the new [`BeanRegistrar` support in the upcoming Spring Framework 7 line](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=Wxd_FvEDe0Q)
-   I also looked at the [new `JmsClient` in Spring Framework 7, too](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=Y0N6t_gR3Ys&t=200s)
-   want more enterprise-grade distributed job scheduling? `@Scheduled` is fine, but this blog [looks at some more durable alternatives](https://medium.com/@ujjawalr/avoid-scheduled-for-background-tasks-use-this-instead-47647c2bda6c)
-   you might also try Temporal as a way to get distributed job scheduling, and this video looks to demonstrate it\]([https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=zVfOa7z-Gdo](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=zVfOa7z-Gdo))
-   I am elated i got to sit down and record [a discussion with our friends over at Heroku](https://www.heroku.com/podcasts/codeish/spring-boot-with-guest-josh-long/)
-   [Gradle 9.0 is out!](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=GJGBPwfhpdo) - congrats to the Gradle team!
-   Rapheal De Lio has a nice blog on [using Redis' new semantic caching to caching with Spring AI and Redis](https://medium.com/redis-with-raphael-de-lio/semantic-caching-with-spring-ai-redis-1bf0db4473d1) - in effect this caches both the data and a vector of the data so that fuzzy matches will still get cached. Genius!
