# This week in Spring: July 12th, 2011

_Engineering | Josh Long |  July 13, 2011 | 0 Comments_

Welcome back to another installment of "This Week in Spring." Today saw a new sunrise, and - more importantly - the release of vSphere 5, [the next step in cloud infrastructure](http://www.vmware.com/cloud-infrastructure/business-critical-applications.html)!

My head's still buzzing after the excitement that accompanied this morning's launch.  
This - and the [recent release of vFabric 5](http://blog.springsource.com/2011/06/14/announcing-vfabric-5/) - represent the next stage in cloud innovation, and a huge part of taking your applications to production, and to the cloud, with Spring.
