# This Week in Spring - August 8th, 2023

_Engineering | Josh Long |  August 08, 2023 | 0 Comments_

Hi, Spring fans! Welcome to another installment of *This Week in Spring*! I'm *baaaaack* in the USA for the first time in more than two months, and it feels great to be home!

I just concluded a tour of Asia, visiting

-   Taipei, Taiwan
-   Sydney, and the Blue Mountains, in Australia
-   Seoul, S. Korea
-   Singapore
-   Jakarta and Bali, Indonesia
-   Kuala Lumpur, Bali, and Penang, Malaysia
-   Tokyo, Japan

i did public appearances in most of these cities, and it was a lot of fun. Thank you everyone that came out, and I can't wait to see you again soon!

I feel like I time-traveled. You remember during lockdown there was that odd sense of being stuck in March of 2020? Or perhaps earlier in some countries? I sorta feel like that. I'm back in San Francisco, and it feels like early June and we've got the whole summer ahead. But we don't, do we? It was a fun summer, but I want to make the best use of the dwindling few days in summer as I can!

And on that note, my friends, we've got a lot to get into this week, and - if I'm honest - I'm feeling a little clumsy on my desktop ergonomic keyboard after months of using my laptop keyboard exclusively - so let's dive right into it!
