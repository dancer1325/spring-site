# This Week in Spring - March 26, 2013

_Engineering | Josh Long |  March 26, 2013 | 0 Comments_

Welcome to another installment of *This Week in Spring*! This week I'm in *chilly* (brrr!) London, England and Paris, France, for [Devoxx UK](www.devoxx.com/display/UK13/Home) and [Devoxx FR](www.devoxx.com/display/FR13/Home) and - *tonight* - I gave a talk at *Skills Matter* for the London Spring User Group. What a pleasant experience. If you're in France and want to talk Spring, don't hesitate [to ping me](http://twitter.com/starbuxman).
