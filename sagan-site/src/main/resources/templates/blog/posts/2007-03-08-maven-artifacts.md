# Maven Artifacts

_Engineering | Ben Hale |  March 08, 2007 | 0 Comments_

At long last I can finally say that [SPR-1484](http://opensource.atlassian.com/projects/spring/browse/SPR-1484) is resolved. Opened on 20 November 2005 with 121 votes, 63 watchers, and even its own [anti-ticket](http://opensource.atlassian.com/projects/spring/browse/SPR-2599) this issue rates as one of the all time biggies. In the last 10 minutes I've uploaded the maven artifacts for Spring 1.2.9 to our local repo and you should be seeing them replicated onto the central maven repo in the next 6 hours or so.

You will notice on the Spring [JIRA](http://opensource.atlassian.com/projects/spring) issues such as [SPR-2704](http://opensource.atlassian.com/projects/spring/browse/SPR-2704), [SPR-1383](http://opensource.atlassian.com/projects/spring/browse/SPR-1383), and [SPR-3198](http://opensource.atlassian.com/projects/spring/browse/SPR-3198). What this means is that we're not done improving our builds and and we will continue to respond to the community. In fact, we've got some really cool new tools that I can't wait to show off in upcoming posts.

So, now that SPR-1484 is officially resolved, please open any issues you have with the POMs in new issues and I'll keep an eye out for them.
