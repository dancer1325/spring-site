# Spring HATEOAS 2.1.5, 2.2.2 and 2.3 RC1 released

_Releases | Oliver Drotbohm |  April 12, 2024 | 0 Comments_

I am happy to announce the availability of Spring HATEOAS 2.1.5, 2.2.2 and 2.3 RC1. The primary content of the release is the upgrade to the latest Spring Framework releases as they contain [a fix for a CVE](https://spring.io/blog/2024/04/11/spring-framework-6-1-6-6-0-19-and-5-3-34-available-now-including-fixes-for).

The releases will be included in the Spring Boot service and RC release next week.
