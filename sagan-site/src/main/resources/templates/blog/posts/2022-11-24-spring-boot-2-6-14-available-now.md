# Spring Boot 2.6.14 available now

_Releases | Stéphane Nicoll |  November 24, 2022 | 0 Comments_

On behalf of the team and everyone who has contributed, I'm happy to announce that Spring Boot `2.6.14` has been released and is now available from Maven Central.

This release includes [39 bug fixes, documentation improvements, and dependency upgrades](https://github.com/spring-projects/spring-boot/releases/tag/v2.6.14). Thanks to all those who have contributed with issue reports and pull requests.

### [](#end-of-life)End of life

It's just over a year since the release of Spring Boot 2.6.0 which means that, [as per our open source support policy](https://spring.io/projects/spring-boot#support), 2.6.14 is the last release that's planned in the 2.6.x line. Releases may be made in the future, but only in response to a [commercial support](https://tanzu.vmware.com/spring-runtime) request.

As [previously announced](https://spring.io/blog/2022/05/24/preparing-for-spring-boot-3-0), Spring Boot 2.7 will be the last minor in the 2.x line. As such, we’ve extended our open source support for 2.7.x release by an additional 6 months until November 2023. In addition, [commercial support](https://tanzu.vmware.com/spring-runtime) for Spring Boot 2.7 has also been extended and is available until February 2025.
