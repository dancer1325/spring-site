# Spring Framework 5.2.10 and 5.1.19 available now

_Releases | Stéphane Nicoll |  October 27, 2020 | 0 Comments_

On behalf of the team and everyone who has contributed, I am pleased to announce that Spring Framework `5.2.10` and `5.1.19` are available now.

Spring Framework `5.2.10` includes [27 fixes and improvements](https://github.com/spring-projects/spring-framework/releases/tag/v5.2.10.RELEASE). Spring Framework 5.1.19 includes [16 selected fixes and improvements](https://github.com/spring-projects/spring-framework/releases/tag/v5.1.19.RELEASE).

Stay tuned for Spring Framework 5.3 GA and follow-up Spring Boot releases `2.1.18`, `2.2.11`, `2.3.5`, and `2.4.0-RC1`!
