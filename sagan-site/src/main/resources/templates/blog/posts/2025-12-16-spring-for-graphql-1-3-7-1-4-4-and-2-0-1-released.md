# Spring for GraphQL 1.3.7, 1.4.4 and 2.0.1 released

_Releases | Brian Clozel |  December 16, 2025 | 0 Comments_

I am pleased to announce that the December Spring for GraphQL maintenance releases are now available on Maven Central.

Spring for GraphQL [1.3.7 ships a single dependency upgrade](https://github.com/spring-projects/spring-graphql/releases/tag/v1.3.7) and will be shipped this week with Spring Boot 3.4.13. Spring for GraphQL [1.4.4 ships 6 fixes and upgrades](https://github.com/spring-projects/spring-graphql/releases/tag/v1.4.4) and will be shipped this week with Spring Boot 3.5.9. Spring for GraphQL [2.0.1 ships 9 fixes and upgrades](https://github.com/spring-projects/spring-graphql/releases/tag/v2.0.1) and will be shipped this week with Spring Boot 4.0.1.

As always, [you can check the official Spring release calendar for upcoming releases](https://spring.io/projects#release-calendar).
